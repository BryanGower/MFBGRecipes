# Carrot Cake

## Ingredients


### Dry

- 1 1/2 cup oat flour
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 cup walnuts (≈50g)
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt

### Wet

- 1 1/2 cup carrots (shredded)
- 3 eggs
- 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
- 1/4 cup honey
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (nonfat)

### Frosting

- 1/2 cup coconut milk (from can)
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (nonfat)
- 3 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. Make the Frosting:
In a bowl, mix coconut milk, greek yogurt, chia seeds,
maple syrup, cinnamon, and a pinch of salt. Stir well,
cover, and place in the fridge for at least 30–40
minutes so the chia seeds set. (For thicker frosting,
make it an hour+ ahead.)
2. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
3. Mix the dry ingredients:
In a large bowl, whisk together oat flour, cinnamon,
walnuts, baking soda, baking powder, and salt until
well combined.
4. Add wet ingredients:
Stir in shredded carrots, eggs, vanilla, honey, and
greek yogurt. Mix until smooth and combined.
5. Bake:
Pour batter into a greased or parchment-lined
9x9-inch baking dish. Bake for 12–15 minutes until
a toothpick comes out clean.
6. Cool, frost & store:
Let the cake cool fully, then spread frosting
evenly on top. Store in the fridge to keep it set
and fresh.
