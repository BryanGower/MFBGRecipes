# Protein Bars No. 2

## Ingredients

- PROTEIN BARS NO.2 INSTRUCTIONS

### Bars

- 1 1/2 cups oat flour
- 1/2 cup peanut butter
- 2 scoops protein powder
- 3/4 cup greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 1/4 cup + 1 tbsp honey

### Chocolate Topping

- 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- Pinch of salt

### Additional Toppings

- Flaky salt & honey

## Instructions

1. Mix:
Oat flour, peanut butter, protein powder, greek
yogurt, honey and salt. Combine until smooth.
2. Shape Bars:
Place the mixture into a square 9x9 baking dish
lined with parchment paper. Press down evenly to
form a dough-like base. Keep some extra dough for
topping (optional).
3. Make Chocolate Topping:
In a separate bowl, mix melted coconut oil, cocoa
powder, and maple syrup until smooth. Pour over
the protein bar base and spread evenly.
4. Top, Freeze & Cut:
Drizzle with honey and sprinkle with a pinch
of salt. Freeze for 15 minutes until the
chocolate sets. Then, cut into squares and
place it back in the freezer (so you don’t
have to cut it when it’s fully frozen and really
hard).

## Notes

You can customize these based on what
your goals are. Cut the calories down and
lower the fat by removing some of the
peanut butter adding more greek yogurt,
oats, or honey. Mix and match ingredients to
get the right texture and flavor.
