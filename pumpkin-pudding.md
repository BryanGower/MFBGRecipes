# Matcha Bowl

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp matcha
- 1 banana
- 1 cup greek yogurt (nonfat of any
- type)
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 2 tbsp chia seeds
- Pinch of salt

### Matcha Pudding

- 2 9 g 5 g 2 3 g 2 6 2 5 0 M I N S 2

## Instructions

1. Blend the matcha, banana, greek yogurt,
vanilla protein powder, chia seeds, and a
pinch of salt together in a high-powered
blender until thick, smooth, and fully
combined.
2. Pour the mixture into a bowl or jar and
place it in the fridge to chill for at least 45
minutes, or overnight, so it sets into a
creamy pudding consistency.
3. Top with sliced banana, oats, or any
fruit or toppings you like right before
serving for extra texture and flavor.
