# Granola

## Ingredients

- 3 cups oats
- 1 cup nuts/seeds
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup honey
- 1/4 cup coconut oil
- 1/2-3/4 cup vanilla protein powder
- (about 2-3 scoops)

## Instructions

1. Mix Dry:
Combine oats, nuts/seeds, cinnamon, and
salt. (Optional: Add protein powder.)
2. Melt & Mix:
Melt honey and coconut oil in a pan over
heat, then mix into the dry ingredients.
3. Bake:
Spread the mixture on a parchment-lined
baking sheet. Smash mixture down on the
sheet to get granola clusters. Bake at 350°F
(175°C) for 15 minutes.
4. Store:
Let it cool and harden completely. Break into
pieces and store in a container on the counter.

## Notes

Make sure to watch it so it doesn’t over-bake.
You can make the recipe without the protein
power.
