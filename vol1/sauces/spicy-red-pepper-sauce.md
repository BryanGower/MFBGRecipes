# Spicy Red Pepper Sauce

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 1/2 red bell pepper
- 1/2 jalapeño (adjust for spice
- preference)
- 2 cloves garlic
- 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
- Juice of 1/2 lime (~1 tsp)
- 1 tsp paprika
- Salt, to taste
- Chili flakes, for topping
- Notes:
- This sauce is perfect on the
- Cucumber Salad or
- Wrap.

## Instructions

1. Blend all ingredients together
until smooth and creamy.
2. Taste and adjust. If the sauce is
too thin, mix in a little more greek
yogurt after blending or let it rest
in the fridge to thicken.
