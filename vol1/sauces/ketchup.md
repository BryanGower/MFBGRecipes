# Ketchup

## Ingredients

- 1 (28 oz) can of tomato puree
- 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 cup honey
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a
saucepan over medium heat and
whisk together. Add more honey
if you want it sweeter.
2. Bring to a simmer, then
reduce heat to low. Let it simmer
for 20-30 minutes, stirring
occasionally.
3. Let it cool, then blend until
smooth.

## Notes

This Ketchup is perfect for the
Chicken Nugget recipe.
Also, if you don’t use it all you can
store it in the fridge for 2-3 weeks.
