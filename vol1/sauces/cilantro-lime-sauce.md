# Cilantro Lime Sauce

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cottage cheese (low-fat or
- any type)
- Juice of 2 limes
- 1 cup fresh cilantro, uncut (a
- generous handful)
- 3–4 garlic cloves
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Water or more lime juice (if needed
- to thin the sauce)
- Notes:
- This sauce is perfect on the
- Wrap,  Tacos, &
- Sweet Potato Nachos.

## Instructions

1. Blend all ingredients together
until smooth and creamy.
2. Taste and adjust.
