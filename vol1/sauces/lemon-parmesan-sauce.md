# Lemon Parmesan Sauce

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese (non-fat)
- Juice of 1-2 lemons
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
- 1/2 tsp thyme
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Pinch of salt & pepper
- Optional: 2 tsp honey
- Notes:
- This sauce is perfect on the
- Spaghetti Squash
- Salad.

## Instructions

1. Blend all ingredients together
until smooth and creamy. If it’s too
thin, mix in a little more greek
yogurt after blending.
2. Taste and adjust.
