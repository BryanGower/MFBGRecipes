# Peanut Sauce

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp peanut butter
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce (no
- preservatives or additives)
- 2 tbsp water
- Salt and pepper, to taste

### Peanut Butter Sauce


## Instructions

1. In a bowl, whisk all the ingredients
together until smooth and combined.

## Notes

Instead of adding water you can
add more soy sauce. Double the
recipe for extra sauce. This sauce
is perfect on  lettuce
wraps.
