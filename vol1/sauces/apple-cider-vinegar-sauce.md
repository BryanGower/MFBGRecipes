# Apple Cider Vinegar Sauce

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese (4% or
- any type)
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 tbsp + 1 tsp honey
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 1/4 tsp paprika
- Salt & pepper, to taste
- Notes:
- This sauce is perfect for the
- Apple Cider Vinegar
- Salad and  Flatbread.

## Instructions

1. Blend all ingredients together until
smooth and creamy.
2. Taste and adjust sweetness with
honey or tanginess with lemon and
vinegar.
