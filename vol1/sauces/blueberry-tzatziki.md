# Blueberry Tzatziki

## Ingredients

- 1 large cucumber
- 1 cup blueberries
- 2 cups greek yogurt (nonfat)
- 1/2 cup fresh dill
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1–2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/4 tsp salt

## Instructions

1. In a blender, combine the blueberries, greek yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, and salt. Blend until smooth.
2. Shred the cucumber and chop the dill.
3. Add the shredded cucumber and chopped dill to the blended mixture. Stir to combine.
4. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
