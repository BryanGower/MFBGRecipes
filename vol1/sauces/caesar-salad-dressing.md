# Caesar Salad Dressing

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1 egg yolk
- Juice from 1 lemon
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/4 tsp mustard powder
- 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
- 2 tbsp greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 3 tbsp parmesan cheese
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 1/4 tsp salt

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, whisk together olive
oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt,
pepper, mustard powder, egg
yolk, apple cider vinegar, and
parmesan.
2. Stir in the greek yogurt. Add more
Greek yogurt or parmesan if it’s too
tangy.
3. Mix well and serve over a
Salad.
