# Banana Bars

## Ingredients


### Base Layer

- 3/4 cup oatmeal
- 2 ripe bananas, mashed
- 2 scoops protein powder (1/2 cup)
- Dash of cinnamon
- Pinch of salt

### Chocolate Layer

- 2 tbsp coconut oil, melted
- 1/4 cup cocoa powder
- 1 tbsp maple syrup

### Top Layer

- 1/4 cup peanut butter
- 1 ripe banana, mashed
- 1 tbsp maple syrup

## Instructions

1. Prepare the Base:
Mix oatmeal, mashed bananas, protein powder,
cinnamon, and salt until well combined. Press this
mixture firmly into an 8x8 or 9x9-inch square pan.
2. Add the Chocolate Layer:
Combine melted coconut oil, cocoa powder, and
maple syrup until smooth. Pour over the base layer
and spread evenly.
3. Set the Chocolate:
Refrigerate the pan for about 5 minutes, or until the
chocolate layer hardens.
4. Prepare the Top Layer:
Mix and mash peanut butter, banana, and maple
syrup until smooth. It might seem dry initially, but
keep mixing—it will become creamy.
5. Add the Top Layer:
Spread the peanut butter mixture over the
hardened chocolate layer evenly.
6. Final Chill:
Place the pan in the refrigerator or freezer
for 30-45 minutes, until everything is firm.
7. Serve:
Cut into 15 bars. Store them in the
refrigerator for a softer texture or in the
freezer for a firmer bite.
