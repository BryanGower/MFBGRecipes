# Date Caramel Apple Cake

## Ingredients

- 20 pitted dates
- 2 apples
- 1 cup milk (or water)
- 1 1/2 cups oat flour
- 1 tbsp cinnamon
- Salt
- Honey (for topping)

## Instructions

1. Preheat the Oven:
Preheat to 350°F (175°C).
2. Make Date Caramel:
Soak dates in warm milk (or water) until
soft, then mash into a caramel-like
paste. Add a pinch of salt.
3. Cook Apples:
Chop apples and cook in a pan on
medium-high heat with cinnamon and a
pinch of salt. Add half the caramel and
mix until coated and tender.
4. Make Dough:
Combine oat flour with the remaining
caramel sauce to form a dough. Reserve
some for the top crust.
5. Assemble:
Press most of the dough into a greased
cake pan, spread the apple mixture on top,
and crumble the reserved dough over it.
6. Bake & cool:
Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 40 minutes. Let it
cool and drizzle with honey before serving.
