# Raspberry Cheesecake Brownies

## Ingredients

- RASPBERRY CHEESECAKE BROWNIES INSTRUCTIONS
- BROWNIE LAYER:
- 1 1/2 cups baked sweet potato (or a
- can of pumpkin)
- 1 scoop protein powder (vanilla or
- chocolate)
- 1/4 cup peanut butter
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder
- 3/4 cup oat flour
- 3/4 cup chocolate chips (
- chocolate chips pg. 38)
- Pinch of salt
- Optional: 2 tbsp maple syrup for more
- sweetness & texture.
- CHEESECAKE LAYER:
- 1 egg
- 1 cup cottage cheese (low-fat)
- 1 cup greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 3 tbsp maple syrup
- Shake of cinnamon
- RASPBERRY SAUCE:
- 2 cups raspberries
- 2 tbsp honey

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (176°C):
Wrap sweet potato in foil and bake until
completely tender (35-40 mins).
2. Mix Brownie Batter:
Stir all brownie layer ingredients until smooth.
Spread into a 9×9 baking dish.
3. Blend Cheesecake Layer:
Blend all cheesecake ingredients and pour over
the brownie batter. Spread evenly.
4. Bake:
Bake for 20-25 minutes, then cool for an hour
before chilling in the fridge.
5. Make Raspberry Sauce:
Add and mix raspberries and honey in a pan on
high heat until jammy. Let it cool in the fridge.
6. Assemble:
Pour raspberry sauce over the cooled brownies,
spread it out, and serve.

## Notes

Mix the base well—KA chips melt in (due to the
warmth of the sweet potato) and keep it moist.
If it’s dry, add a bit of maple syrup.
