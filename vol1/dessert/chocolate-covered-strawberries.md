# Chocolate-Covered Strawberries

## Ingredients

- 20 strawberries
- CHOCOLATE SAUCE:
- 1/2 cup coconut oil (melted)
- 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 2 tbsp maple syrup

## Instructions

1. Wash and dry the strawberries
thoroughly. Any moisture will prevent the
chocolate from sticking.
2. In a bowl, whisk together the melted
coconut oil, cocoa powder, and maple
syrup until smooth.
3. Dip each strawberry into the chocolate
mixture, coating it evenly.
4. Place the dipped strawberries on a
parchment-lined tray.
5. Refrigerate or freeze for 2–5 minutes
until the chocolate hardens.

## Notes

If the sauce is too thick, add more
coconut oil. Also, if you have leftover
chocolate sauce, you can store it in the
freezer. Once frozen, cut it up to make
homemade chocolate chips.
You can add protein powder to the
chocolate sauce to increase protein.
