# Chocolate Caramel Cookies

## Ingredients


### Cookie Base

- 1.5 cups oat flour
- 2 scoops protein powder (~3/4 cup, chocolate or vanilla)
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder
- 3/4 cup greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 3 tbsp maple syrup (add a little more if dry)
- Shake of salt

### Caramel

- 4 Medjool dates
- 1 medium banana
- 1/4 cup water (or milk)

### Chocolate Sauce

- 2 tbsp cocoa powder
- 1 tbsp coconut oil (melted)

## Instructions

1. Make the Caramel: Soak dates and banana in hot water for 15 minutes, then blend until smooth. Set aside.
2. Make the Cookie Base: Mix oat flour, protein powder, cocoa powder, greek yogurt, maple syrup, and salt until a dough forms.
3. Shape into 15 cookies and place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
4. Press a small indent in each cookie and fill with caramel.
5. Make the Chocolate Sauce: Mix cocoa powder and melted coconut oil.
6. Drizzle chocolate sauce over cookies.
7. Freeze for 30 minutes or refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.

## Notes

Store in freezer or fridge.
