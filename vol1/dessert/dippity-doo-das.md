# Dippity-Doo-Das

## Ingredients

- 1 cup greek yogurt (low-fat, the
- brand Fage works well)
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (or
- 2 tbsp honey)

## Instructions

1. Mix greek yogurt, protein powder (or
honey), and berries in a bowl.
2. Form into 6 globs on parchment paper
and freeze for 30 minutes.
3. Make Chocolate Sauce:
Melt coconut oil and mix in cocoa powder
until smooth. Make sure the chocolate is
cooled before dipping.
1.5 cup berries
CHOCOLATE SAUCE
1/2 cup coconut oil
1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
Optional: a dash of maple syrup
for sweetness
4. Dip frozen yogurt globs into the chocolate
sauce. It will harden instantly.

## Notes

They are best to eat right after you dip them.
If you have leftover chocolate sauce, freeze it
and cut it up later for  chocolate
chips.
