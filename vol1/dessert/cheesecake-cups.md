# Cheesecake Cups

## Ingredients

- BASE LAYER:
- 1 cup oat flour
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (≈1/3 cup)
- Pinch of salt (optional: cinnamon)
- CHEESECAKE FILLING:
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese (low-fat or any type)
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- CHOCOLATE SAUCE:
- 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 tbsp melted coconut oil
- 1 tsp maple syrup

## Instructions

CHEESECAKE CUPS INSTRUCTIONS 1. Make the Base:
Mix all base layer ingredients in a bowl. Shape into small
cups on a parchment-lined tray. Throw in the freezer to
harden while you make the filling.
2. Make the Filling:
Blend cottage cheese, greek yogurt, vanilla, and maple
syrup until smooth. Pour into the cups. Freeze until firm
(about 20 minutes).
3. Make the Chocolate Sauce:
Mix cocoa powder, melted coconut oil, and maple syrup
until smooth.
4. Assemble:
Once the cheesecake cups are firm, drizzle with
chocolate sauce. Return to the freezer to set
(about 20 mins).
5. Eat & Store:
Eat and store the rest in the fridge.

## Notes

If the mixture is too dry and won’t come together,
add 1 tablespoon of yogurt at a time until it forms
a dough ball.
