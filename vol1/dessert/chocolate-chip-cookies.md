# Chocolate Chip Cookies

## Ingredients

- 1 cup vanilla protein powder (≈3 scoops)
- 1 1/4 cup oat flour
- 3/4 cup almond flour
- 1/4 cup peanut butter
- 1/4 cup + 3 tbsp milk (2% or any type)
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1/4 cup honey
- Pinch of Salt
- 1/3 cup Chocolate Chips
- CHOCOLATE CHIPS:
- 2 Tbsp coconut oil
- 5 Tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder

## Instructions

1. Make the  Chocolate Chips:
In a small bowl, mix the melted coconut oil
with the unsweetened cocoa powder until
well combined.
Place the mixture in the freezer until set (30
mins or less).
2. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
3. Make the Cookie Dough:
In a bowl, combine protein powder, oat
flour, almond flour, and salt. Mix well.
Add peanut butter, milk, and maple syrup;
mix until combined.
Fold in the honey.
4. Prepare the Chocolate Chips:
Once the chocolate mixture has hardened in
the freezer, remove it and chop it into small
pieces to create your  chocolate
chips. Add to the mixture immediately.
5. Combine and Bake:
Fold the  chocolate chips into the
cookie dough.
Scoop dough into 10 balls and place on a
lined baking sheet.
Bake for 7 minutes. Do not over-bake — pull
them out while still softened.
NOTES
If the dough feels too dry, add extra milk 1
teaspoon at a time. Remove cookies from the
oven while they’re soft to keep them from
drying out.
