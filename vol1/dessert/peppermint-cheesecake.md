# Peppermint Cheesecake

## Ingredients


### Chocolate Crust

- 2 cups oat flour
- 2 eggs
- 3 tbsp coconut oil
- 1/4 cup honey
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder
- Pinch of salt

### Cheesecake Filling

- 2 eggs
- 2 cups cottage cheese (non-fat or any type)
- 2 cups greek yogurt (2% or any type)
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
- 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

### Chocolate Frosting

- 1/2 cup coconut cream (from can)
- 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Make the Chocolate Crust:
In a bowl, combine oat flour, eggs, melted coconut oil, honey,
cocoa powder, and a pinch of salt. Mix until well combined,
then press firmly into the bottom of a springform pan to form
the crust. If the mixture isn’t fully coming together, add water 1
teaspoon at a time until it combines, or add a touch more
coconut oil or honey to help it bind.
3. Prepare the Cheesecake Filling:
In a blender or food processor, blend cottage cheese, greek
yogurt, eggs, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and peppermint
extract until smooth and creamy. Start with 1/2 teaspoon
peppermint extract and adjust to taste if needed. Pour the
filling evenly over the crust.
4. Bake:
Place the cheesecake in the oven and bake for 28-30 minutes,
until the center is mostly set. Crack the oven door open
slightly and let the cheesecake cool inside for 30 minutes.
5. Make the chocolate frosting:
While the cheesecake bakes, whisk together coconut cream,
cocoa powder, maple syrup, and salt until smooth and thick.
Place in the fridge to chill and thicken.
6. Chill:
Remove from the oven and place the cake in the refrigerator
to chill for a few hours, or until fully set.
