# Protein Bars

## Ingredients


### Protein Bars Instructions


### Bars

- 1 1/2 cups oat flour
- 1/2 cup peanut butter
- 1 1/2 scoops vanilla protein powder
- 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
- 2 tbsp honey
- Pinch of salt

### The Chocolate Topping

- 1/3 cup coconut oil
- 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- Sprinkle of salt
- Honey (optional, for topping)

## Instructions

1. Mix:
Oat flour, peanut butter, protein powder, melted
coconut oil, and honey. Combine until smooth.
2. Shape Bars:
Place the mixture into a square 9x9 baking dish
lined with parchment paper. Press down evenly
to form a dough-like base.
3. Make Chocolate Topping:
In a separate bowl, mix melted coconut oil and
cocoa powder until smooth. Pour over the
protein bar base and spread evenly.
4. Top, Freeze & Cut:
Drizzle with honey and sprinkle with a
pinch of salt. Freeze for 15 minutes until
the chocolate sets. Then, cut into squares
and place it back in the freezer (so you
don’t have to cut it when it’s fully frozen
and really hard).

## Notes

If it’s really dry, you can add a little bit of
water or more honey to get a better
texture. (you can half this with water or
add more honey to lower fat content)
