# Cheesecake

## Ingredients

- THE CRUST:
- 3 cups oat flour
- 1/4 cup honey
- 3 tablespoons coconut oil
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- THE CHEESECAKE FILLING:
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 2 cups cottage cheese (2%)
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon lemon zest (optional, for extra flavor)
- RASPBERRY SAUCE TOPPING (OPTIONAL):
- 2 cups frozen raspberries
- 2 tablespoons honey

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Make the crust:
Add all crust ingredients to a food processor and blend until well
combined. If mixing by hand, combine thoroughly in a bowl (be
prepared to mix well). Press the dough firmly into a springform
pan. It will be tough, so use some muscle to compact it evenly.
3. Make the cheesecake filling:
Blend all cheesecake filling ingredients in a blender until smooth.
Pour the mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
4. Bake:
Bake for 28-30 minutes, or until set.
5. Cool gradually (to prevent cracking):
Turn off the oven and crack the oven door open. Let the
cheesecake sit inside for 30 minutes before removing it.
6. Refrigerate:
Once cooled, transfer the cheesecake to the fridge to
chill completely.
7. Make the raspberry sauce (optional):
In a saucepan over high heat, combine frozen
raspberries and honey. Cook, stirring, until the mixture
thickens into a jam-like texture. Let it cool slightly
before topping the cheesecake.
