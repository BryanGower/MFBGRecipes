# Chocolate Coconut Balls

## Ingredients

- 1 cup greek yogurt

## Instructions

CHOCOLATE COCONUT BALLS INSTRUCTIONS 3. Form the Balls:
Roll the chilled mixture into balls and place
on parchment paper. Chill again (15-20
mins).
4. Dip the Balls:
Coat each ball in chocolate sauce.
5. Finish & Store:
Let the chocolate set until firm, then enjoy!
Store leftovers in the fridge to keep them
fresh.
2.5 scoops vanilla protein powder
1.5 cups shredded coconut
Optional: honey (for extra sweetness)
THE CHOCOLATE COATING:
1 cup cocoa powder
1/2 cup coconut oil (melted)
1/4 cup maple syrup
1. Mix the Coconut Ball Base:
Combine greek yogurt, protein powder,
shredded coconut, and honey (if using).
Chill in the freezer (just firm enough to
roll!).
2. Make the Chocolate Sauce:
Mix cocoa powder, melted coconut oil,
and maple syrup. Adjust to taste if
needed.
