# Marshmallows

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup water, divided
- 2 tbsp grass-fed beef gelatin
- 1/2 cup honey
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- A shake of cinnamon
- A pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. Prepare the Gelatin:
In a mixing bowl, combine 1/4 cup warm
water and 2 tablespoons gelatin. Stir until
dissolved.
2. Prepare the Syrup:
In a saucepan over medium heat, mix the
remaining 1/4 cup water, 1/2 cup honey, and a
pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, stirring
continuously, and let it boil for about 5
minutes.
3. Combine and Whip:
Carefully pour the hot syrup into the gelatin
mixture. Add 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract and
a shake of cinnamon. Using a mixer, start on
low speed to combine, then increase to high
and whip for approximately 8 minutes, until
the mixture forms stiff peaks.
4. Set the Marshmallows:
Quickly transfer the fluffy mixture into a
parchment-lined container, spreading it evenly.
Refrigerate for about 30 minutes to set.
5. Cut and Serve:
Once set, remove from the container and cut
into 20 pieces. Store them in the fridge.
