# Lettuce Wraps

## Ingredients

- 1 lb chicken breast
- 1–2 cups edamame (deshelled)
- 2 cups purple cabbage, shredded
- 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
- Green onion, chopped (for topping)
- Salt, pepper, and any seasonings you like
- 2 tbsp peanut butter
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (non-fat or any type)
- 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce (no preservatives or additives)
- 2 tbsp water (for texture)
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Iceberg lettuce

### Lettuce Wraps


## Instructions

Slice your purple cabbage, red onion, and green
onion. Add the cabbage, red onion, edamame,
and cooked chicken to the bowl.
4. Mix it All Together:
Pour the sauce over the chicken and veggie
mixture. Toss until everything is evenly coated.
5. Assemble the Wraps:
Spoon the mixture into your iceberg lettuce
leaves. Top with chopped green onion.
1. Make the  PB Sauce:
In a bowl, combine the peanut butter, non-fat
Greek yogurt, apple cider vinegar, paprika,
cayenne pepper, minced garlic, soy sauce,
water, salt, and pepper. Mix until smooth and
creamy. Set aside.
2. Cook the Chicken:
Dice the chicken breasts really small. Season
with salt, pepper, and any other spices you like.
Cook until done, then transfer to a large bowl.
