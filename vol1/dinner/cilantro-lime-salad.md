# Cilantro Lime Salad

## Ingredients

- 1 lb chicken breast
- 1-2 tomatoes
- 1 sweet potato
- 1/2 red onion
- 1 cup corn
- 1 cup black beans
- 4 cups kale
- 1 avocado

### Sweet Potatoes

- Paprika, Salt and pepper & Avocado oil

### Chicken

- Chili powder, Paprika, Garlic powder,
- Italian seasoning, & Salt and pepper

### Cilantro Lime Sauce

- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 cup cottage cheese
- Juice of 2 limes
- A generous handful of fresh cilantro
- 3–4 garlic cloves
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Water (if needed to thin sauce)

## Instructions

1. Roast Sweet Potatoes:
Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss diced
sweet potatoes with avocado oil, paprika, salt,
and pepper. Roast for 25-30 minutes, flipping
halfway through.
2. Make Sauce:
Blend sauce ingredients together. Add water if
too thick.
3. Cook Chicken:
Season chicken with chili powder, paprika, garlic
powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Cook in
avocado oil over medium-high heat for 5–7 minutes per
side. Let rest, then cut into cubes.
4. Assemble Salad:
Combine kale, chicken, roasted sweet potatoes,
tomatoes, red onion, corn, black beans, and sauce in a
large bowl. Toss well.
