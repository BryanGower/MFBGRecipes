# Steak & Sweet Potato Bowl

## Ingredients


### With Apple Cider Vinegar Sauce


### Apple Cider Vinegar Sauce

- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese (4% Good Culture)
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 tbsp + 1 tsp honey
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Paprika (generous shake)
- Pinch of Salt and pepper

### The Chicken

- 1 lb chicken breast
- Avocado or coconut oil (for cooking)
- Seasonings: 2-3 tsp paprika, 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper,
- 1/2 tsp chili seasoning, 1/2 tsp cumin, 1 tsp garlic
- powder, 1/2 tsp onion powder, & a generous amount of
- salt and pepper

### Roasted Sweet Potatoes

- 1 large sweet potato, diced
- Seasonings: 1/2 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp pepper, 1/2 tsp
- paprika, 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning, & 1 tbsp avocado oil

### Salad Base

- 4 cups kale
- 1/2 red bell pepper
- 1/2 yellow bell pepper
- 1/2 red onion
- 1 carrot

## Instructions

INSTRUCTIONS 1. Prep Work:
Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) for the sweet
potatoes.
Cut the veggies for the salad (bell peppers, onion, and
carrot).
2. Make the  Sauce:
Blend together all the sauce ingredients until smooth
and well combined.
3. Roast the Sweet Potatoes:
Toss diced sweet potatoes with salt, pepper, paprika,
Italian seasoning, and avocado oil.
Spread evenly on a baking sheet and bake for 20-30
minutes, flipping halfway through.
4. Cook the Chicken (my way):
Cut the chicken breasts in half lengthwise.
Coat the chicken with the seasoning blend (paprika, cayenne,
chili, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper).
Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat with avocado or coconut
oil.
Once hot, add the chicken and sear for about 3-5 minutes per
side.
Reduce heat, cover the skillet, and let it sit for 5 minutes to lock in
moisture.
5. Assemble the Salad:
Toss the chopped kale, bell peppers, red onion, and carrot with
the MFBG sauce.
Add the roasted sweet potatoes and top with sliced chicken.
