# Burgers

## Ingredients


### Burgers Instructions


### Burgers

- 2 large red onions
- 1 lb (16 oz) lean ground beef
- White cheddar cheese
- Salt & pepper

### Ketchup

- 1 (28 oz) can tomato puree
- 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 cup honey
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Make the  Ketchup:
Combine all ketchup ingredients in a saucepan
over medium heat and whisk together. Bring to a
simmer, then reduce heat to low and let it simmer
for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Let it cool,
then blend until smooth. Store any extra in the
fridge — it’ll last 2–3 weeks.
2. Slice the red onions into large, half-inch circles.
Keep the rings intact to use as the “buns”.
3. Form the ground beef into burger patties and
season with salt, pepper, or your preferred
seasonings.
4. Cook the patties on high heat. Add white
cheddar cheese on top and let it melt. Remove
from the pan.
5. Add the onion slices in the same pan you
cooked the meat in, mixing them in the leftover
burger flavors. Flip and cook for about 5 minutes
per side.
6. Assemble the  Burgers.
