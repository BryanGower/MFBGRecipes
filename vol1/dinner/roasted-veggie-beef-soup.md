# Roasted Veggie & Beef Soup

## Ingredients

- 1 butternut squash, peeled and cubed (≈ 4 cups)
- 3 tomatoes
- 2 cups carrots, chopped
- 1 head broccoli, chopped
- 1 whole head garlic, halved
- 1/2 onion
- 2 tbsp avocado oil
- Seasonings: 1 tbsp paprika, 2 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp cayenne
- pepper, 1 tsp salt, & 1 tsp black pepper
- SAUTÉED VEGETABLES & GROUND BEEF
- 1/2 onion, chopped
- 1 red bell pepper, chopped
- 1 can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 lb lean ground beef
- 1 tbsp avocado oil
- Seasonings: 2 tsp paprika, 1 tsp oregano
- 1 tsp thyme, 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper, 1/2 tsp salt, &
- 1/2 tsp black pepper

### Soup Base

- 4-6 cups broth (beef, chicken, or veggie — add more if needed)
- 3 cups kale, chopped

### Optional Topping

- Grated parmesan

## Instructions

ROASTED VEGGIE & BEEF SOUP INSTRUCTIONS 1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Toss the
squash, tomatoes, carrots, broccoli, garlic, and
onion with avocado oil and seasonings, then
spread on a baking sheet. Roast for 35–40
minutes, until soft.
2. While the veggies roast, heat avocado oil in a
large pot over medium heat. Add onion and red
bell pepper with the spices, cook until softened,
then stir in the ground beef. Once browned, mix
in the cannellini beans.
3. Blend the roasted vegetables until smooth,
adding a splash of broth if needed. Stir the
blended mixture into the pot with the beef,
then add the broth and bring to a gentle
simmer.
4. Stir in the chopped kale and cook just until
wilted. Adjust broth for desired consistency,
then serve hot with grated parmesan if you’d
like.
