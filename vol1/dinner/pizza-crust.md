# Pizza Crust

## Ingredients


### Pizza Crust Instructions


### Crust

- 1 lb cups shredded chicken
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp paprika

### Optional Toppings

- Red sauce
- Parmesan
- Mozzarella
- Mushrooms
- Red onion
- Cottage cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450°F (232°C).
2. Make the crust:
In a bowl, mix together the crust ingredients.
3. Form the crust shape:
Press the mixture onto a parchment-lined
baking sheet, shaping it into a flat circle.
4. Bake:
Place the chicken crust in the oven for about
15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden
brown and firm. Keep an eye on it.
5. Add toppings and Bake:
Spread sauce over the crust, sprinkle cheese,
and add your toppings. Put it back in the oven
for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted
and the toppings are cooked.
6. Let it cool:
Let the pizza cool slightly and then slice it.
