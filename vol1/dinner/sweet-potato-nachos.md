# Sweet Potato Nachos

## Ingredients


### Potato Base

- 2-3 sweet potatoes
- 1 lb lean ground beef
- Meat seasonings (garlic powder, onion powder,
- paprika, chili powder, salt, pepper)
- Grated parmesan (for topping)

### Optional Toppings

- Black beans
- Guacamole
- Pico ge gallo
- Lettuce

### Cilantro Lime Sauce

- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (nonfat)
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese (2%)
- Juice of 1-2 limes
- A generous handful of fresh cilantro
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. Cut and soak sweet potatoes:
Take the skin off of the sweet potatoes with a
peeler, slice them into thin circles, and soak them in
ice water for 20 minutes. Remove them from the
water and dry them really well. Toss with olive oil,
salt, and pepper.
3. Bake sweet potatoes:
Place the sweet potatoes on a baking sheet lined
with parchment paper and bake for 15 minutes, flip,
bake another 15 minutes, and then broil for 2
minutes.
4. Make Kate’s  Sauce:
Blend together the greek yogurt, cottage cheese, lime
juice, cilantro, paprika, salt, pepper, and garlic. Double the
recipe if you like a LOT of sauce and if it’s too thick, add a
little water or more lime juice.
5. Cook ground beef:
Mix your seasonings into the ground beef and cook it in a
pan with olive oil (or avocado oil) on medium to high heat.
Once it’s completely cooked to your liking, remove it from
the stove.
6. Assemble:
Top your crispy sweet potatoes with grated parmesan,
ground beef,  sauce, and garnish with cilantro and
lime.
