# Lasagna

## Ingredients

- 2 large sweet potatoes

### Cheese Sauce

- 2 eggs
- 2 cups cottage cheese (2% of any type)
- 1 cup grated parmesan (save 1/4 for topping)
- Seasonings: 1/4 tsp paprika, 1/4 tsp garlic powder, 1/4
- tsp onion powder, pinch of salt & pepper

### Meat Mixture

- 32 oz (2 lb) lean ground turkey (ground beef or
- chicken work too)
- 1 yellow onion, chopped
- 4 tomatoes, chopped (or 6oz can of tomato paste)
- 3 garlic cloves (minced)
- 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
- Seasonings: 1-2 tsp paprika, 1/4 chili powder, 1 tbsp
- Italian seasoning, 1 tsp salt, & 1/2 tsp black pepper

## Instructions

LASAGN 2 9 g 1 3 g 1 8 g 3 1 7 INSTRUCTIONS
1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (218°C).
2. Slice sweet potatoes into thin circles (1/4inch).
Thinner slices bake faster.
3. Make the cheese sauce:
Mix or blend 2 eggs, 2 cups cottage cheese, 3/4 cup
parmesan, paprika, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion
powder. Set aside.
4. Sauté the vegetables and cook the meat:
Heat a pan over medium heat. Add onions, minced
garlic, paprika, chili powder, salt, pepper, oregano, basil,
thyme, and rosemary. Once softened, add diced
tomatoes and mushrooms. Mash the tomatoes slightly to
create a sauce-like texture. Add ground turkey and cook
until no longer raw. Drain some of the liquid if necessary.
5. Assemble:
Grease a 9×13” glass pan with avocado oil.
Layer as follows: sweet potato slices, cheese sauce,
meat mixture. Repeat layers, finishing with cheese
sauce, meat, and 1/4 cup of parmesan for topping.
6. Bake:
Cover with foil and bake for 40-45 minutes.
7. Broil:
Remove the foil and broil for a few minutes to crisp the
top.

## Notes

If you want a smaller serving cut the recipe in half and
use a 9x9 pan.
