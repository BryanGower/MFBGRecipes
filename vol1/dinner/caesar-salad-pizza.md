## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (232°C).
2. Make the Caesar dressing:
Whisk or blend all the dressing ingredients together.
Place in fridge to thicken.
3. Make the crust:
Mix the chicken, parmesan, eggs, salt, pepper, garlic
powder, and paprika together. Press the mixture
into 4 flat circles on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
Bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden and firm.
4.5 (1.5 lbs) cups shredded chicken breast (about
3 breasts)
1/2 cup grated parmesan
3 eggs
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp paprika
CAESAR SALAD DRESSING
2 tsp olive oil
1 tbsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp apple cider vinegar
1 egg yolk
1 minced garlic clove
2 tbsp grated parmesan
1/3 cup Greek yogurt (low-fat)
Pinch of salt and pepper
TOPPINGS
1/2-1 head Romaine Lettuce
Parmesan
Radicchio (optional)
Crutons (optional)
4. Assemble the pizza:
Let the crust cool slightly. Cut your lettuce, add
Caesar dressing, and toss to coat. Pile the salad on
top of the crust and finish with desired toppings.
