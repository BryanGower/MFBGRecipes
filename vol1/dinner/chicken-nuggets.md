# Chicken Nuggets

## Ingredients

- 1 lb (16 oz) ground chicken or lean ground turkey
- 1/2 cup grated parmesan
- 1/2 cup almond flour
- 1/2 cup oat flour
- 2 eggs

### Seasonings

- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Prepare the meat mixture:
In a bowl, combine the ground chicken (or turkey)
with 1/4 cup of grated parmesan, paprika, salt,
pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Mix
thoroughly and set aside.
3. Set up the dipping bowls:
In one bowl, crack 2 eggs and whisk until fully
combined. In another bowl, mix 1/2 cup almond
flour, 1/2 cup of oat flour, the remaining 1/4 cup of
parmesan, and the same seasonings (paprika, salt,
pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder).
4. Form the nuggets:
Shape small portions of the ground meat
into 15-16 balls. Dip them into the egg
mixture, then coat them in the
flour/parmesan mixture. Pat them down
into a nugget shape.
5. Bake, flip, & broil:
Place the nuggets on the prepared baking
sheet, (use a wire rack if you have one),
and bake for 10 minutes. Flip the nuggets
and bake for another 4 minutes to crisp the
bottoms. Broil for 2-3 minutes to get the
tops crispy.

## Notes

They will get even more crispy with a spray
of avocado oil before baking.
