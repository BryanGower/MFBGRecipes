# Shepherd's Pie

## Ingredients


### Potato Layer

- 2 large white sweet potatoes (or any type)
- 1 cup low-fat cottage cheese
- 3/4 cup grated parmesan (save 1/4 cup extra for topping)
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- Salt to taste

### Meat Layer

- 1 lb lean ground beef
- 1 yellow onion, diced
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 cup green peas
- 1 cup diced carrots
- 1/4 cup tomato paste
- 1/4 to 1/2 cup bone broth
- 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp paprika
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Instructions

SHEPHERD’S PIE INSTRUCTIONS
1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Prepare the potatoes:
Peel and chop the Japanese sweet potatoes, then boil for
20 minutes until fork-tender.
3. Cook the meat layer:
Heat a cast-iron skillet with olive oil. Sauté onion and
garlic until fragrant. Add ground beef, season, and cook
until browned. Stir in bone broth, tomato paste, peas, and
carrots. Remove from heat and set aside.
4. Mash the potatoes:
Drain potatoes and blend with cottage cheese, garlic
powder, salt, and 1/2 cup parmesan until smooth.
5. Assemble the pie:
Spread the mashed potatoes over the meat layer.
Top with 1/4 cup parmesan.
6. Bake:
Bake for 20-30 minutes until golden.
7. Cool:
Let it cool before serving.
