# Spaghetti Squash Pasta

## Ingredients

- 1 large spaghetti squash
- 1 yellow onion, diced
- 1 cup cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
- 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 cups spinach
- 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 2 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- Pinch of salt
- 1 tbsp olive oil (or avocado/coconut oil)

### The Creamy Sauce

- 1 cup cottage cheese (4% or any type)
- 3/4 cup unsweetened coconut milk
- 2 garlic cloves
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Roast the Spaghetti Squash:
Preheat the oven to 445°F. Cut the spaghetti
squash in half and stab it with a fork. Drizzle
with olive oil and season lightly with salt and
pepper. Roast for 35-40 minutes, until tender.
2. Make the Sauce:
Blend cottage cheese, coconut milk, garlic, salt
and pepper until smooth and creamy.
3. Sauté the Veggies:
Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
Add onion, garlic, Italian seasoning, paprika,
salt, and pepper, and sauté until fragrant. Stir
in mushrooms and tomatoes and cook until
softened. Add spinach and stir until wilted.
4. Combine Everything:
Add the roasted spaghetti squash into the pan
with the veggies. Pour the creamy sauce over
the top and mix everything well.
