# Flatbread

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cottage cheese (any type)
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F
(175°C).
2. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
3. Line a baking sheet with
parchment paper and pour the batter,
spreading evenly.
4. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until
firm and slightly golden.
5. Let it cool slightly before using.
6. Fill the inside with whatever you
want, my MFBG Spicy Pepper
Sauce takes it to the next level.
