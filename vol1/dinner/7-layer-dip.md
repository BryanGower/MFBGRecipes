# 7-Layer Dip

## Ingredients

- LAYER 1 – BEANS
- 3 cups black beans
- (2 cans, drained, rinsed, and
- blended until smooth)
- LAYER 2 – SHREDDED
- 3 cups (1 lb) shredded
- chicken (cooked and
- seasoned as desired)
- LAYER 3 – PARMESAN
- 1 1/2 cups shredded
- Parmesan cheese
- LAYER 4 – GUACAMOLE
- 3 avocados (mashed)
- Juice of 2 limes
- Chopped cilantro
- 1 diced tomato
- 1/2 red onion, diced
- Salt, pepper, and garlic
- (to taste)
- 7-LAYER DI 2 7 g 1 4 g 2 2 g 3 2 0 P O S T /  1 H R 9
- Layer 1 – Beans
- Spread blended black beans evenly in the bottom of a
- 7×11-inch baking dish.
- Layer 2 – Shredded Chicken
- Cook and season chicken as desired, then layer it over
- the beans.
- Layer 3 – Parmesan
- Sprinkle Parmesan evenly over the chicken.
- Bake at 350°F for 15 minutes until warm and slightly
- melted.

### Instructions

- LAYER 5 – PICO DE GALLO
- 3 tomatoes, diced
- 1 red onion, finely chopped
- Juice of 1 lime
- Chopped cilantro (to taste)
- Salt (to taste)
- LAYER 6 –  SAUCE
- Blend together:
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese
- Juice of 1 lime
- Generous handful of fresh
- cilantro
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 2 garlic cloves
- Salt and pepper (to taste)
- LAYER 7 – LETTUCE
- A layer of finely cut fresh
- lettuce
- Optional: Make my
- Chicken Chips recipe for
- dipping into your 7-layer dip.
- Layer 4 – Guacamole
- Spread guacamole evenly over the Parmesan layer.
- Layer 5 – Pico de Gallo
- Evenly distribute pico de gallo over the guacamole.
- Layer 6 –  Sauce
- Pour and spread the sauce over the pico layer.
- Layer 7 – Lettuce
- Top with fresh lettuce.
