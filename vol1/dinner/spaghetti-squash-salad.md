# Spaghetti Squash Salad

## Ingredients

- 1 lb chicken thighs
- 1-2 spaghetti squash
- 1 large sweet potato, cubed
- 1 bunch asparagus
- 1/4 cup pine nuts

### Lemon Parm Sauce

- 1/4 cup greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 1/4 cup cottage cheese (non-fat)
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
- 1/4 tsp thyme
- 1/4 tsp paprika
- Pinch of salt
- Pinch of pepper
- Optional: 2 tsp honey

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (204°C).
2. Roast Veggies:
Halve squash, remove seeds, drizzle with olive
oil, season, and roast until tender (30–40
minutes). Shred the squash with a fork. Cube
the sweet potatoes, season, and roast for 25-30
minutes. Roast the asparagus for 10-20
minutes.
3. Toast Pine Nuts:
Toast the pine nuts in a pan over medium heat
until light brown.
4. Cook Chicken:
Marinate the thighs and sear in them in a skillet
until crispy and cooked through.
5. Make Sauce (double it if you want extra sauce):
Blend the Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, lemon
juice, garlic, parmesan, salt, and pepper until
smooth.
6. Assemble:
Toss the spaghetti squash, roasted veggies,
chicken, and pine nuts with the sauce.
