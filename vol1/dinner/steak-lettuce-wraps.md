# Steak Lettuce Wraps

## Ingredients

- 1 lb steak (sirloin)
- 1 head iceberg lettuce
- 1 medium sweet potato
- 1/2 cup corn
- 1/4 cup red onion
- 1 avocado

### Cilantro Lime Sauce

- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (nonfat)
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese (2%)
- Juice of 1 lime
- A generous handful of fresh cilantro
- 1 garlic clove
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (204°C).
2. Roast Veggies:
Cut sweet potato into cubes and drizzle lightly
with olive oil. Season with salt, pepper, and
paprika. Bake for 25–30 minutes, tossing
halfway through for even roasting.
Optional – roasted corn:
Add corn to a parchment-lined baking sheet,
season with chili powder, salt, and pepper, and
roast for 15 minutes until lightly browned.
3. Cook Steak:
Season steak with salt, pepper, and your go-to
spices. Heat olive oil in a hot skillet and cook the
steak to your liking. Let it rest for a few minutes
before slicing or cubing.
4. Make Sauce:
Blend all sauce ingredients. Add water or more
lime juice for desired consistency.
5. Build the wraps:
Lay down two or more iceberg leaves per wrap,
add steak, veggies, and sauce, then wrap it up
— fold the sides in first, then roll it tightly from
front to back.
