# Edamame Hummus

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups edamame (boiled until soft)
- 1 can chickpeas (about 1 1/2 cups cooked, drained and rinsed)
- 1/3 cup tahini
- 1/4 cup lemon juice
- 1 tbsp olive oil (add more if desired)
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1/4–1 cup cold water (start with 1/4, add more as needed)

## Instructions

1. Boil edamame until soft and rinse chickpeas.
2. Blend or food process edamame, chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, paprika, and salt until smooth.
3. Stream in cold water a little at a time until desired consistency.
4. Taste and adjust seasoning.
