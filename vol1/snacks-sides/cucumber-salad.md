# Cucumber Salad

## Ingredients


### Cucumber Mixture

- 2 cucumbers, sliced
- 1/2 shallot (topping, optional)
- Red pepper flakes (topping)

### Red Pepper Sauce

- 3/4 cup greek yogurt (non-fat)
- 1/2 red bell pepper
- 1/2 jalapeño (adjust for spice preference)
- 2 cloves garlic
- 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
- Juice of 1/2 lime (~1 tsp)
- Generous amount of paprika
- Chili powder, to taste
- Salt, to taste

## Instructions

1. Make the Sauce:
Blend all sauce ingredients until smooth
and creamy. Adjust spice as needed. If the
sauce is too thin, mix in a little more greek
yogurt after blending or let it rest in the
fridge to thicken.
2. Assemble the Salad:
Add sliced cucumbers to a bowl. Mix in the
Spicy Red Pepper Sauce until the
cucumbers are coated (you’ll have extra
sauce left, store it in the fridge for later). Top
with red pepper flakes and shallots.
