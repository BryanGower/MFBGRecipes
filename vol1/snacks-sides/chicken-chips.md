# Chicken Chips

## Ingredients

- 1 lb cooked shredded chicken
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup parmesan
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
