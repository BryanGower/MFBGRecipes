# Waffles

## Ingredients

- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup oat flour
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (low-fat)
- 1 tbsp honey
- Cinnamon
- Pinch of salt
- Optional: 1/4 tsp baking powder
- (for rise)
- Notes:
- The batter will be liquidy; that’s how it’s
- supposed to look. Trust the process.

## Instructions

1. In a bowl (or blender) add and
mix all the ingredients.
2. Grease the waffle iron with
coconut oil.
3. Pour the batter into the waffle iron
and cook (3-5mins).
4. Top the waffles with your desired
toppings.
