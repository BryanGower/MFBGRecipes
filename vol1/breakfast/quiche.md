# Quiche

## Ingredients

- 2 cups almond flour
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tbsp fresh thyme
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper

### The Vegetable Filling

- 1 cup onion, diced
- 1 cup mushrooms, diced
- 1 handful spinach
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Pinch of salt and pepper

### The Egg Mixture

- 6 eggs
- 1 cup cottage cheese (low-fat)
- 3/4 cup freshly grated parmesan
- Salt & pepper, to taste

## Instructions

INSTRUCTIONS QU ICH 4 5 M I N S 8
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a food processor, combine the crust ingredients:
almond flour, egg, coconut oil, thyme, paprika, garlic
powder, salt, and pepper. Press the mixture into a pie pan
and bake for 5 minutes to set.
3. In a pan, sauté the onions, mushrooms, spinach, garlic,
salt, and pepper until softened.
4. In a bowl, whisk together the egg filling
ingredients: eggs, cottage cheese, parmesan,
minced garlic, salt and pepper.
5. Spread the sautéed vegetables evenly over
the crust, then pour the egg mixture on top.
6. Bake for 30 minutes or until the center is set.
7. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
