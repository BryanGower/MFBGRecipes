# Pancakes

## Ingredients

- 1 banana
- 3 egg whites (≈ 1/3 cup)
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 1/2 tbsp honey
- Dash of cinnamon
- Pinch of salt

### Optional Toppings

- Greek yogurt
- Fruit

## Instructions

1. Mash and whisk all the ingredients
together (or blend until everything is
smooth and combined).
2. Pour the batter into a greased pan
with coconut oil on high heat, then
lower to medium once the batter is
in. Flip after a few minutes.
3. Top your pancakes with fruit
Greek yogurt, or whatever you
like.
