# Chicken Skillet

## Ingredients


### Chicken

- 2 lbs chicken breast tenders
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 2 tsp paprika
- 1 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 1/2 tsp salt

### Sauce

- 1/4 cup tomato paste
- 3 tbsp soy sauce (or tamari, additive free)
- 3/4 cup coconut milk (canned, unsweetened)
- 3/4 cup greek yogurt (nonfat)
- 1/3 cups lime juice
- 2 tbsp ginger, shredded (grated)
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 jalapeño, finely chopped

### Toppings

- Green onion, finely chopped
- Red bell pepper, finely diced

## Instructions

1. Season the chicken:
In a large bowl, coat chicken with paprika, salt, and
pepper.
2. Sear:
Heat a large skillet over medium-high. Add coconut
oil. Sear chicken 4–5 minutes per side until browned
and cooked through. Remove to a plate.
3. Deglaze with lime juice:
Lower heat to medium. Pour in lime juice and
scrape up browned bits.
4. Build flavor:
Add garlic, jalapeño, and ginger; sauté 1–2 minutes
until fragrant.
5. Add tomato paste:
Stir in and cook 1–2 minutes.
6. Soy + coconut milk:
Cut the heat and add the soy sauce, stirring to
combine. As the sauce cools slightly, whisk
together the coconut milk and greek yogurt in a
separate bowl. Slowly pour the coconut–yogurt
mixture into the pan, stirring continuously until
smooth and orange.
7. Finish chicken:
Return the chicken into the pan, mixing it in
with the sauce until fully coated.
8. Top + serve:
Garnish with green onion and red bell pepper.
Serve with rice, quinoa, or veggies.
