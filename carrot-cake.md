# Carrot Cake Cookie Dough

## Ingredients

- 1 cup oat flour
- 1 cup carrot (shredded)
- 1 cup chickpeas
- 2 bananas
- 2 scoops vanilla protein
- powder (≈3/4 cup)
- 1/4 cup walnuts (≈25g)
- 2 tbsp honey
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
- 1/4 tsp salt

## Instructions

1. Add the oat flour, shredded carrot,
chickpeas, bananas, vanilla protein
powder, walnuts, honey, cinnamon,
vanilla extract, and salt into a food
processor. Blend until everything is
fully combined and the mixture
reaches a thick cookie-dough texture.
2. Chill in the fridge for 30 minutes
to firm up and achieve the best
texture before serving. Store any
leftovers in the fridge.
