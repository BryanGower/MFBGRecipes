# Pumpkin Cheesecake

## Ingredients


### The Crust

- 3 cups oat flour
- 1/4 cup honey
- 3 tbsp coconut oil
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp salt

### The Cheesecake Filling

- 2 eggs
- 2 cups greek yogurt (nonfat)
- 2 cups cottage cheese (2%)
- 1 can (15 oz) unsweetened pumpkin puree
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg (optional)

### Pumpkin Frosting

- 1/2 can coconut cream or coconut milk
- 1/2 can pumpkin puree
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Make the crust:
Add all crust ingredients to a food processor and blend
until well combined. (If mixing by hand, stir thoroughly
and press firmly into a springform pan.) The dough will be
thick, so use some muscle to pack it evenly. If it’s a bit
crumbly, add a little extra coconut oil until it holds
together.
3. Make the cheesecake filling:
Blend all filling ingredients until smooth. Pour over the
crust in the springform pan.
4. Bake:
Bake for 28–30 minutes, or until set.
5. Cool gradually (to prevent cracking):
Turn off the oven, crack the door open, and let the
cheesecake sit inside for 30 minutes before
removing.
6. Refrigerate:
Once cooled, move the cheesecake to the fridge
and chill completely.
7. Make the pumpkin frosting:
Whisk together coconut cream, pumpkin puree,
maple syrup, cinnamon, and salt until smooth and
thick. Spread evenly over the chilled cheesecake
before serving.
