# Energy Balls

## Ingredients

- 8 dates (≈120 g / 1 cup),
- pitted
- 1 cup old-fashioned oats
- 3 tbsp cacao nibs
- 1 banana (≈100 g)
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp salt

## Instructions

1. Add dates, oats, cacao nibs,
banana, cinnamon, and salt to a
food processor.
2. Blend until mixture comes
together into a sticky dough.
3. Roll into 7 balls with your
hands.
4. Chill in fridge 15–20 min to
set (optional).
