# Bananas Foster

## Ingredients

- 2 bananas
- 3 large medjool dates
- Water or milk (for soaking dates)
- 2 tbsp Honey
- Pinch of Salt
- greek yogurt (optional side)

## Instructions

1. Soak dates:
Deseed the dates and soak them in hot
water (or milk) for 15 minutes, then mash or
blend into a caramel-like sauce.
2. Cook bananas:
Heat a pan with coconut oil on medium
heat and add your cut bananas to the pan.
When they have browned (1-2 minutes), flip
them.
3. Add date sauce and honey:
After flipping your bananas, add about half
of your date sauce to the pan, topping
each banana slice. Drizzle honey on top
and cook for about 3 minutes until you feel
like the sauce has incorporated into your
bananas.
4. Plate it:
Add your bananas and the remaining sauce
in the pan to a plate. Optional: sprinkle with
salt. Place a spoonful of greek yogurt with
your extra date sauce on top.
