# Apple Pie Cookies

## Ingredients

- 3 large Honeycrisp apples (about 6 cups, diced)
- 3 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- Pinch of salt
- 3 scoops vanilla protein powder (1 cup)
- 1 cup old-fashioned oats
- 1 cup oat flour
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp honey
- Juice of 1/2 lemon (2 Tbsp — add zest if you
- want extra flavor)
- Optional (for texture):
- 1/4 cup milk (any kind — add only if the mixture
- is too dry)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line a baking
sheet with parchment paper.
2. Cook apples:
Heat and mix diced apples with maple syrup,
cinnamon, lemon juice, lemon zest (if desired)
and salt for 6–8 min until soft.
3. Make dough:
Mix protein powder, honey, oat flour, oats,
and greek yogurt. It’ll be thick and wet.
4. Combine:
Fold in the apples. Scoop into 10 balls and place
on the baking sheet.
5. Bake for 12 minutes.

## Notes

Don’t over-bake.
Pull them out while still soft-ish! Store in a
container on the counter (they’ll get moist after
a day) or freeze and microwave for 10–15
seconds.
If overly dry — add a splash of milk.
If overly wet — add a bit more oat flour.
