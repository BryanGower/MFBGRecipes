# Harvest Salad

## Ingredients


### Roasted Veggies

- 1 large sweet potato, diced
- 2 cups brussels sprouts, halved
- 1/2 red onion, sliced
- 3 tbsp pumpkin seeds
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Seasonings: 2 tsp paprika, 2 tsp onion
- powder, 2 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp
- cumin, 1/2 tsp salt, & 1/2 tsp pepper

### Salad Base

- 1 lb shredded chicken
- 1 cup quinoa
- 1 cup kale, chopped
- 1 apple, chopped

### Tahini Sauce

- 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1 1/2 tbsp honey
- 1/4 cup tahini
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (nonfat)
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/4 tsp salt
- A few cracks of black pepper
- Water, as needed for thinning

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
3. Dice the sweet potato, halve the brussels sprouts,
and slice the red onion. Spread on a baking sheet with
pumpkin seeds.
4. Drizzle with olive oil, then season with paprika, onion
powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat.
Roast for 25–30 minutes, tossing halfway, until golden
and tender.
5. Make the sauce: in a bowl, whisk together apple cider
vinegar, honey, tahini, greek yogurt, garlic, black pepper,
and salt. Add water slowly until creamy and pourable.
6. Assemble the salad: in a large bowl, combine roasted
veggies, quinoa, kale, apple, and shredded chicken.
Drizzle with  Tahini Sauce, toss, and serve.
