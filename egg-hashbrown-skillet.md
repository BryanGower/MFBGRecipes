# Curry Stir Fry

## Ingredients

- 1 lb chicken breast, diced
- 1 yellow onion, sliced
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 orange bell pepper, sliced
- 1 head of broccoli, chopped
- 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp minced ginger
- 1 can (1 ½ cups) unsweetened coconut milk
- Juice of 1 lime (for topping)

### Seasonings

- 1 tbsp curry powder, 2 tsp paprika, 1/2 tsp
- cumin, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp cayenne
- pepper, 1/2 tsp black pepper, salt to taste
- until the veggies are tender. Cover the pan, if needed,
- to speed up the cooking process.

## Instructions

1. Heat a large pan with coconut oil or olive oil.
2. Add diced chicken breast seasoned with salt and
black pepper into the pan. Cook ad stir for about 5
minutes (it won’t be fully cooked yet). Dispose of any
water left in the pan. Remove the chicken and set
aside.
3. Toss in the sliced onions, bell peppers, broccoli,
minced garlic, and minced ginger. Add a bit more
coconut oil.
4. Stir everything together, then add the curry
powder, paprika, cumin, turmeric, black pepper,
cayenne, and salt.
6. Add and mix the partially cooked chicken back to
the pan. Then, add in the coconut milk. Stir well and
let everything cook for another 5–10 minutes until the
chicken is fully cooked. Taste and adjust salt.
7. Top it with fresh lime juice.
