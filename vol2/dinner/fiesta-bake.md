# Fiesta Bake

## Ingredients


### Bake

- 1 lb chicken breast (shredded)
- 1 large sweet potato, cubed
- 1 onion sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 medium tomatoes, diced (about 2 cups)
- 1 1/2 cups corn (1 can, drained)
- 1 1/2 cups black beans (1 can, drained & rinsed)
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese (for topping,≈8oz)
- Seasonings: 1 tbsp smoked paprika, 1 tbsp chili
- powder, 2 tsp ground cumin, 2 tsp oregano, 2
- tsp salt, & 1 tsp black pepper

### Optional Toppings

- Avocado Sauce
- or
- Adobo Sauce

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (175°C).
2. Cook the chicken, shred it, and set aside.
3. Cut the sweet potatoes into cubes, slice the onion,
mince the garlic, and cube the tomatoes.
4. In a large bowl, combine shredded chicken, sweet
potatoes, onion, garlic, corn, black beans, tomatoes,
olive oil, fresh cilantro, and all spices. Mix until evenly
coated.
5. Transfer mixture to a 9×13-inch dish and spread
evenly (you may have extra so get another pan or
bigger dish).
6. Bake for 30 minutes or until vegetables are fork
tender.
7. Make the  Avocado or Adobo Sauce
(recipes in sauce section) while it's baking in the oven.
8. Pull the bake out of the oven and add mozzarella on
top and bake another 10 minutes, until melted.
9. Remove bake from oven and garnish with fresh
cilantro, avocado slices, lime juice, and drizzle with
Adobo Sauce before serving.
