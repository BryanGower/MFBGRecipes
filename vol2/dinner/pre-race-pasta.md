# Pre-Race Pasta

## Ingredients


### Meat And Veggie Mixture

- 1/2 lb lean ground turkey
- 1/2 yellow onion, diced
- 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
- 2 cups spinach
- 3 tsp olive oil (divided)
- 1 tsp salt, plus more to taste
- 1 tsp pepper, plus more to taste

### Tomato Sauce

- 5 tomatoes, quartered
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp fresh basil (or 1 tbsp dried)
- 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 2 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp salt, plus more to taste
- 1 tsp pepper, plus more to taste
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese (2%)

### Pasta

- Fresh semolina pasta (340g or 3/4 lb)

## Instructions

-RACE PASTA
1. Make the Sauce
Add tomatoes, garlic, basil, Italian seasoning, paprika, salt, and
pepper to a blender. Blend until smooth, then pour into a
saucepan. Bring to a light boil, reduce to a simmer, and cook
for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally until thickened.
2. Cook the Meat and Veggies
While the sauce simmers, heat 2 tsp olive oil in a skillet over
medium heat. Cook the turkey with salt and pepper until
browned, then remove and set aside. Add 1 tsp olive oil, sauté
onion and mushrooms until soft, then add spinach until wilted.
Return the turkey, mix well, and remove from heat.
3. Cool the Sauce
Once the sauce has thickened, remove it from heat and let it
cool for 5–10 minutes so it doesn’t curdle when you add the
cottage cheese.
4. Cook the Pasta
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the fresh
pasta according to package instructions.
5. Reserve Pasta Water
Scoop out 1/2 cup of pasta water mid-cook to loosen the
sauce later. Drain pasta once cooked.
6. Blend and Combine
Blend 1/2 cup cottage cheese with a few spoonfuls of cooled
sauce until smooth, then stir it back into the pan until
creamy. If it curdles, just re-blend the sauce until smooth
again.
7. Assemble
Add the sauce to the turkey and veggies, pour in a splash of
pasta water, and toss with the pasta until combined.
8. Finish
Serve warm and season to taste.
