# Chicken Tenders

## Ingredients

- CHICKEN:
- 1 1/2 lbs chicken tenderloins (about 12
- tenders)
- BREADING:
- 1 cup (≈3 slices) sourdough
- 2 tbsp parmesan, shredded
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 1/2 tsp paprika
- Salt & pepper to taste
- OTHER:
- 2 eggs
- 1 tbsp avocado oil spray

## Instructions

3 7 g 1 0 g 1 8 g 3 2 2
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (204°C).
2. Make breadcrumbs:
Bake 2-3 slices of sourdough until crisp, then
blend into fine crumbs.
3. Set up two bowls:
Bowl 1: Beat 2 eggs with a fork.
Bowl 2: Mix breadcrumbs, parmesan, garlic
powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
4. Bread the tenders:
Dip each chicken tender in egg, then coat in
breadcrumb mix.
5. Bake the tenders:
After breading, spray both sides of each
chicken tender with avocado oil spray. Place on
a wire rack over a baking sheet. Bake for 5
minutes, flip, and bake another 5 minutes.
6. Finish:
Switch oven to broil for ~3 minutes until golden
and crispy.
