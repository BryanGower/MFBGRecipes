# Stuffed Acorn Squash

## Ingredients

- 2 acorn squash, halved and seeds removed
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 1 lb lean ground turkey (93%)
- 1/2 onion, diced
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 cups mushrooms, finely diced
- 1/4 cup walnuts (≈25g), chopped
- 1 cup kale, chopped
- 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Pinch of cayenne pepper (optional, for spice)
- Salt and pepper (to taste)
- 1/4 cup parmesan, shredded
- 1 tbsp honey
- Red pepper flakes, to taste

### Stuffed Squash


## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 420°F (215°C). Cut the acorn squash in
half, scoop out the seeds, and lightly score the flesh. Drizzle
with 2 tsp honey and season with paprika, salt, and pepper.
Roast cut-side up for 35–40 minutes, until tender.
2. While the squash roasts, heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a pan over
medium heat. Add diced onion and cook until softened. Stir in
garlic and cook until fragrant.
3. Add the ground turkey or beef and cook until browned.
Add mushrooms and cook until tender.
4. Mix in chopped walnuts, rosemary, 1 tbsp honey, and a
pinch of cayenne. Season with salt and pepper, then add
chopped kale and cook until wilted.
5. Remove roasted squash from the oven and fill each half
with the meat and mushroom mixture.
6. Drizzle 1 tbsp honey over the stuffed squash, sprinkle
with red pepper flakes, and top with shredded parmesan.
