# Chicken Noodle Soup

## Ingredients

- 1 small spaghetti squash (≈3 cups)
- shredded
- 1 lb shredded chicken breast (≈3 cups)
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 cup carrots, chopped
- 1 cup celery, chopped
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 5 cups chicken broth
- 1 tbsp olive oil

### Seasonings

- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 2 tsp thyme
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
- 2 tsp pepper

## Instructions

1. Prep the “noodles”: Slice spaghetti squash in
half, scoop out seeds, and place on a baking sheet.
Roast at 400°F for 30–40 min until tender. Use a
fork to shred into “spaghetti” strands. Set aside.
2. Cook and shred:
Season chicken breast with salt and pepper. Sear
in a lightly oiled skillet over medium-high for 3–4
minutes per side then shred and set aside.
3. Sauté veggies: In a large soup pot, heat olive
oil. Add onion, carrots, celery, and garlic. Season
with salt & pepper. Cook until onions are
translucent.
4. Build the broth: Pour in chicken broth. Add
garlic powder, onion powder, thyme, turmeric,
and bay leaves. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a
simmer until carrots are tender (10–12 min).
5. Combine everything: Stir in shredded chicken
and spaghetti squash “noodles.” Simmer 10–15
min to let flavors meld.
6. Taste test: Adjust seasoning if needed.
Remove bay leaves before serving.
