# Chicken Parmesan

## Ingredients


### Tomato Sauce

- 5 tomatoes
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 tsp italian seasoning
- 2 tsp paprika, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp salt,
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- Optional: 1/4 cup freshly cut basil
- (Or use a clean ingredient jar of marinara sauce if
- preferred)

### Breadcrumb Coating

- 1 cup (≈3 slices) sourdough
- 1–2 balls fresh mozzarella (≈4 oz), sliced
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 2 tsp paprika
- Pinch of cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper

### Egg Mixture

- 2 eggs

### Chicken

- 1 lb chicken breast (2 large) sliced lengthwise
- 1/2 tbsp avocado oil spray

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
2. Make the tomato sauce:
Blend tomatoes, garlic cloves, italian seasoning, paprika, onion
powder salt, and pepper until smooth. Simmer on the stove,
stirring occasionally, for 15–20 minutes until the water has
evaporated and the sauce thickens. (Or use a clean-ingredient
jar of marinara sauce.)
3. Make the breadcrumb coating:
Cut a few slices of sourdough and bake at 400°F for about
20–25 minutes, or until hard and crisp. Once cooled, blend into
fine crumbs. In a bowl, combine the sourdough breadcrumbs
with garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cayenne pepper,
salt, and black pepper.
4. Bread the chicken:
Slice each chicken breast in half lengthwise to make cutlets.
Whisk the eggs in a separate bowl. Dip each cutlet into the
egg, then into the breadcrumb mixture. Press to coat well.
5. Bake the chicken:
Place breaded chicken cutlets on a wire rack over a baking
sheet. Spray both sides with avocado oil. Bake 9 minutes, flip,
bake 6 minutes, then switch to broil for 3 minutes to brown.
6. Top it off:
Remove from oven, spoon tomato sauce over each piece, top
with mozzarella slices, and broil again for 5 minutes or until the
cheese is fully melted.
7. Finish:
Remove from the oven, let cool for a few minutes, and enjoy.
Optional: top with fresh basil.
