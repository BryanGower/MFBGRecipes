# Baked Sweet Potato

## Ingredients

- 4 medium sweet potatoes
- 1 lb lean ground beef (90%)
- 1 small yellow onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 cup cottage cheese (2%)
- 2 tsp olive oil
- Optional:
- Avocado or Adobo Sauce
- Green onion, chopped
- Parmesan, shredded

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Wash and score the sweet potatoes, then
place them on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
3. Bake for 45 minutes or until tender.
4. While the potatoes bake, make an optional
Sauce. The Avocado Sauce or Adobo
Sauce are both great options (you can find
them in the sauce section).
5. Dice the onion and mince the garlic. Heat
a large skillet over medium heat and sauté
the onion and garlic until softened.
6. Add the ground beef to the skillet and
cook until browned. Season as desired.
7. Remove the potatoes from the oven, cut
lengthwise, and gently open them.
8. Layer in cottage cheese, ground beef, and
your optional toppings such as
Sauce, green onion, and parmesan.
