# Veggie Wraps

## Ingredients

- 1 cup carrot, shredded
- 1 cup zucchini, shredded
- 2 eggs
- 2 tbsp shredded parmesan
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper

### Optional Fillings

- Beet Sauce (see sauce section)
- Grilled chicken
- Sautéed mushrooms
- Red onion
- Spinach
- Red pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a
baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, mix together the shredded
carrot, shredded zucchini, eggs, parmesan, and
all seasonings until fully combined.
3. Spread the mixture thinly, flatten evenly over
the parchment paper, and smooth out the
surface.
4. Bake for 25–30 minutes, or until the wrap is
firm and lightly golden.
5. Let cool, then fill it with whatever you like —
grilled chicken, sautéed mushrooms, red onion,
spinach, red pepper, or the  Beet Tahini
Sauce (located in the Sauce section).
