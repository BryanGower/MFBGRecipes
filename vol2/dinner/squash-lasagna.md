# Squash Lasagna

## Ingredients


### Base

- 1 butternut squash, sliced very thin (≈750 g)

### Cheese Sauce

- 2 eggs
- 2 cups cottage cheese (2%)
- 3/4 cup parmesan, shredded
- 1/4 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp onion powder
- Pinch of salt & pepper

### Meat Mixture

- 32 oz (2 lb) 93/7 lean ground beef
- 1 yellow onion, diced
- 1/2 cup tomato paste (6oz can)
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
- 1–2 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp chili powder
- 1 tbsp italian seasoning
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper

### Topping

- 1/4 cup grated parmesan

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F (218°C).
2. Slice butternut squash into thin circles (the thinner,
the faster it bakes).
3. Make the cheese sauce: blend or mix cottage
cheese, eggs, 3/4 cup parmesan, paprika, garlic
powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Set aside.
4. Cook the meat mixture: heat a pan over medium
heat. Add diced onion, garlic, paprika, chili powder, salt,
pepper, and italian seasoning. Once softened, add
mushrooms and cook 3–4 minutes until they release
liquid and start to brown. Stir in tomato paste and mix
well. Add ground beef and cook until fully browned.
5. Assemble: grease a 9×13-inch glass pan with
avocado oil. Layer squash slices, cheese sauce, and
meat mixture. Repeat layers, finishing with cheese
sauce, meat, and 1/4 cup parmesan on top.
6. Bake: cover with foil and bake for 50 minutes, until
squash is tender and cooked through.
7. Broil: remove foil and broil a few minutes to crisp
the top.

## Notes

Thinner squash slices = faster bake.
For a smaller serving, cut recipe in half and use a 9×9
pan.
