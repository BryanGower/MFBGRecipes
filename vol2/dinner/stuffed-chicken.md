# Stuffed Chicken

## Ingredients


### Creamy Sauce

- 3/4 cup cottage cheese (2% or any type)
- 1/2 cups greek yogurt (nonfat or any type)
- 1 garlic clove
- Seasonings: 2 tsp Italian seasoning, salt & pepper to taste

### Chicken And Vegetables

- 1 lb of chicken breasts
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 3 cups spinach
- 1 1/2 cups broth (chicken or vegetable)
- Seasonings: 1 1/4 tsp turmeric, 2 tsp paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1
- tsp cumin, & salt & pepper to taste
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for cooking)
- Optional: MASHED SWEET POTATOES
- 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and quartered
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 cup cottage cheese (2% or any type)
- Seasonings: 1/2 cup shredded parmesan
- 1/2 tsp salt, & 1/4 tsp pepper

## Instructions

1. Prepare the Sauce:
In a blender, combine 3/4 cup cottage cheese, 1/2 cup greek
yogurt, 1 garlic clove, 2 tsp Italian seasoning, and salt &
pepper. Blend until smooth. Set aside.
2. Start the Sweet Potatoes (optional):
Peel and quarter the sweet potatoes. Bring a pot of water to
a boil (no need to salt it). Add the sweet potatoes and cook
for 15–20 minutes, until fork-tender. Drain and set aside, or
simply turn off the heat so they stay warm until blending.
3. Season the Chicken:
Rub chicken breasts with 1 1/4 tsp turmeric, 2 tsp paprika, 1
tsp garlic powder, 1 1/4 tsp cumin, plus salt & pepper.
4. Cook the Chicken:
Heat 1 tsp olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Sear
chicken breasts for 3–4 minutes per side until golden but not
fully cooked through. Remove and set aside.
5. Cook the Vegetables:
In the same pan, sauté the onion and minced garlic for 2–3
minutes. Add mushrooms and cherry tomatoes, cooking until
softened. Stir in the spinach until wilted.
6. Simmer with Chicken:
Return chicken to the pan. Pour in 1 1/2 cups broth. Cover and
simmer on low heat for 10 minutes, or until chicken is fully
cooked.
7. Add the Sauce:
Reduce heat to very low and let the pan cool slightly (to
prevent curdling). Stir in the creamy cottage cheese–yogurt
sauce until smooth and combined. Keep heat gentle.
8. Finish the Mashed Sweet Potatoes (Optional):
In a blender, combine the boiled sweet potatoes with 2 garlic
cloves, 1 cup cottage cheese, 1/2 cup parmesan, 1/2 tsp salt,
and 1/4 tsp pepper. Blend until completely smooth and creamy.
9. Serve:
Plate the optional blended sweet potatoes, then top with
chicken and creamy vegetable sauce.
