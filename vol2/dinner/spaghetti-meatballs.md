# Spaghetti & Meatballs

## Ingredients


### Base

- 1 spaghetti squash (≈6 cups)
- 3 red peppers
- 6 roma tomatoes
- 1 yellow onion
- 1 head garlic
- 1/4 cup fresh basil
- 1 tbsp olive oil

### Meatballs

- 1 lb lean ground beef (90%)
- 2 tbsp paprika
- 1 tbsp italian seasoning
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper

### Optional Toppings

- Parmesan, shredded
- Extra fresh basil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Cut tomatoes, red peppers, garlic, and onion into
quarters.
3. Toss them with 1 tbsp olive oil until evenly coated.
4. Cut spaghetti squash in half vertically, score the
inside with a fork, and place face down on a
parchment-lined baking sheet.
5. Bake the squash and vegetables together for 30–
35 minutes or until tender.
6. While they bake, prepare the meatballs:
• Combine ground beef, paprika, italian seasoning,
salt, and pepper.
• Form into ≈10 meatballs.
• Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and sear
each side for 2–3 minutes until browned. Set aside.
7. Transfer roasted vegetables to a blender with
1/4 cup fresh basil and blend until smooth (or use
an immersion blender).
8. Pour the sauce into a saucepan over medium
heat and cook for 5–7 minutes to thicken.
9. Once thickened, cut the heat.
10. Fork out the spaghetti squash strands and add
to the sauce. Mix until evenly coated.
11. Add meatballs and toss gently to combine.
12. Top with shredded parmesan and fresh basil to
finish.
