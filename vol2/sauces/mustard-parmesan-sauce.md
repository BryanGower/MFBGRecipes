# Mustard Parmesan Sauce

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp + 1 tsp whole grain mustard
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan
- 1/4 cup greek yogurt (nonfat,
- plus more for thickness if
- needed)
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, combine lemon
juice, minced garlic, egg yolk,
Parmesan, Greek yogurt, wholegrain mustard, salt, and pepper.
2. Mix well until smooth and
fully combined.
3. If the sauce is too thin, add a little
extra Greek yogurt to thicken.
4. For best flavor and texture,
refrigerate for 20–30 minutes before
serving to let it thicken and meld.
