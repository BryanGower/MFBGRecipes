# Avocado Sauce

## Ingredients

- 2 avocados (250g without skin or core)
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 jalapeño (charred)
- 1/2 cup of cottage cheese
- 1/4 cup cilantro
- 1/4 tsp salt
- Water (if needed for texture)

## Instructions

1. Char the jalapeño:
Use tongs to hold it over an open
flame, turning every 20–30 seconds
until blackened.
Cover in a bowl for 5–10 minutes to
steam.
Peel (optional), then slice, remove
seeds, and add to the blender.
2. Add the jalapeño and all other
ingredients into a blender.
3. Blend until creamy and fully
combined.
4. Add water a little at a time until
you reach your desired consistency.
5. Serve & Store : Use sauce
immediately or store in air-tight
container in fridge. To prevent from
browning, press plastic wrap directly
onto surface of wrap.
