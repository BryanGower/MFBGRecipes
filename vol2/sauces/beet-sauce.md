# Beet Sauce

## Ingredients

- 1–2 beets (≈150g), boiled or roasted
- 2 tbsp tahini
- 3/4 cup cottage cheese (2%)
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 garlic clove
- 1/4 tsp salt
- A few cracks of black pepper
- Water (if needed for texture)

## Instructions

1. Boil or roast beets until tender,
then let cool slightly.
2. Add all ingredients to a blender
and blend until completely smooth.
3. If the mixture is too thick, slowly
add a splash of water while blending
until your desired consistency is
reached.
4. Taste and adjust salt or vinegar
as needed.
