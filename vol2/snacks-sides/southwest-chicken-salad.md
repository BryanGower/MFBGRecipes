# Southwest Chicken Salad

## Ingredients

- FOR THE CHICKEN:
- 1 lb chicken breast
- Seasonings: 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder,
- 1/2 tsp salt, & 1/2 tsp black pepper

### For The Salad

- 1/4 red onion, diced
- 1 1/2 cups black beans (1 can rinsed and drained)
- 2 cups kale, chopped
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
- 2 avocados, diced (250g without skin or core)
- 1 1/2 cups corn (1 can)

### For The  Adobo Sauce

- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (nonfat or any type)
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese (2%)
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- Water (for consistency)
- Seasonings: 1 garlic clove, 1 tsp smoked paprika,
- 1/4 tsp chili powder, pinch cayenne, pinch
- cumin, pinch salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Cook the chicken:
Season with the listed seasonings. Sear in a lightly
oiled skillet over medium-high, 3–4 minutes per side,
until cooked through. Rest, then dice.
2. Prep the salad:
Rinse and drain black beans. Dice onion, chop kale,
halve tomatoes, dice avocados, and chop cilantro.
Drain corn if canned.
3. Make the  sauce:
Blend greek yogurt, cottage cheese, garlic, lime
juice, and spices until smooth; thin with water to
desired consistency.
4. Assemble:
In a large bowl, combine chicken, beans, kale,
tomatoes, avocado, corn, onion, and cilantro. Add
sauce and toss to coat.
5. Serve:
Taste and adjust salt and lime. Garnish with a little
extra cilantro.
