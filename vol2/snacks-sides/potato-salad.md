# Potato Salad

## Ingredients


### Potato Base

- 6 medium red potatoes (≈700g)
- 1 red pepper, diced
- 1/2 red onion, finely diced
- 2 cups celery (≈5 stalks), chopped
- 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- Optional: 2 tbsp parsley, chopped
- sliced green onion (for topping)

### Potato Salad Sauce

- 1 cup greek yogurt (nonfat or any type)
- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 1/2 tbsp whole grain mustard
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp cumin
- 2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper

## Instructions

1. Cook Boil the Eggs
Place the eggs in a pot, cover with water, and bring to a
boil. Once boiling, reduce heat and simmer for 10–12
minutes. Transfer eggs to an ice bath to cool completely.
2. Cook the Potatoes
Cut the red potatoes into bite-sized chunks (slightly
smaller than quarters). Add to a large pot of salted
water and boil until fork tender, about 10–12 minutes.
Drain and let cool.
3. Prep the Vegetables
While potatoes and eggs cool, finely dice the red onion,
and chop the red pepper, celery, dill, and parsley (if
using). Set aside.
4. Make the Sauce
Peel the cooled hard-boiled eggs. Add them to a
blender with the greek yogurt, mustard, lemon
juice, garlic, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper.
Blend until smooth and creamy. If the sauce is too
thick, add a little bit more lemon juice until you
reach your desired consistency.
5. Combine Everything
In a large bowl, add the cooled potatoes, chopped
vegetables, dill, and parsley (if using) . Pour the
sauce over and toss until evenly coated. Taste
test, and adjust with salt if needed.
6. Finish
Top with green onion, chill, and store in the fridge.
