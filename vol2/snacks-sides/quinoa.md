# Quinoa

## Ingredients


### Base

- 1 cup quinoa
- 1/2 yellow onion, diced
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 3 tbsp olive oil

### Tomato Blend

- 4 roma tomatoes
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper

### Toppings

- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro
- 1 lime
- Optional: 1 jalapeño, halved

## Instructions

1. Make the Tomato Blend:
Blend the roma tomatoes with salt and pepper
until smooth. Set aside.
2. Sauté the Aromatics:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Add the diced onion and minced garlic; cook until
translucent.
3. Toast the Quinoa:
Add the dry quinoa to the skillet and cook for 3–5
minutes, stirring often.
4. Add the Tomato Blend:
Pour in the blended tomato mixture. Stir and
cook on medium-high for about 3 minutes, until
the color deepens to a rich reddish-orange.
5. Simmer:
Add 2 cups of warm water. Stir, then place the
halved jalapeño and a few sprigs of cilantro on top.
Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 25–30
minutes, or until all liquid is absorbed.
6. Finish:
Remove the lid, fluff with a fork, and season to taste
with more salt if needed. Top with lime juice and
extra chopped cilantro before serving.
