# Breakfast Biscuits

## Ingredients


### Wet

- 5 eggs
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese (2%)
- 1/4 cup parmesan, shredded (≈20g)
- 1/2 red onion, diced (≈1 cup)
- 1 red pepper, diced (≈1 cup)
- 1 jalapeño, diced (≈1/4 cup)

### Dry

- 2 cups oat flour
- 2 tsp baking powder

### Seasonings

- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp cumin
- 1/4 tsp oregano
- 1/4 tsp thyme
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a
baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together all wet
ingredients until fully combined.
3. In a separate bowl, whisk together all dry
ingredients until evenly mixed.
4. Combine the wet and dry mixtures and stir
until just combined.
5. Let the dough rest for 15 minutes to allow
the oat flour to absorb moisture.
6. Scoop the dough with an ice cream scoop into 7
evenly sized biscuits and place them on the lined
baking sheet.
7. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the tops are lightly
golden and firm to the touch.
8. Let cool for 5–10 minutes before serving.
NOTES:
Biscuits can be stored on the counter for 1–2 days, in
the fridge, or in the freezer. Toast them before
serving for the best texture and flavor.
