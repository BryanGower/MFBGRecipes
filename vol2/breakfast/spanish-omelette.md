# Spanish Omelette

## Ingredients

- 2 yukon potatoes (≈300 g)
- 1/2 onion (≈ 100g)
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 8 eggs
- 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- OPTIONAL TOPPINGS:
- Green onion
- Ketchup

## Instructions

1. Prep vegetables:
Peel the potatoes and thinly slice them, then slice
the onion and mince the garlic.
2. Cook aromatics:
Heat olive oil in a 10-12" round pan; cook onion
and garlic until translucent.
3. Cook potatoes:
Add the potatoes and season with smoked
paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir over medium heat
until evenly coated, then reduce the heat, cover,
and cook until tender.
4. Cool mixture:
Remove from pan and let cool.
5. Whisk eggs:
Whisk eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
6. Combine:
Fold in the cooled potato–onion mixture.
7. Cook omelette:
Add the mixture to a lightly oiled nonstick pan. Cook
uncovered over medium-low heat for 5–7 minutes,
gently loosening the edges with a spatula as it cooks.
Then cover and cook on low for another 3–5 minutes
8. Flip:
Invert onto a plate or lid, slide back into the pan.
9. Finish:
Cover and cook for 3–5 minutes more on low until set.
10. Serve:
Rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving warm.
Or serve it the traditional way — chilled. Let it cool,
then refrigerate and serve cold. Store leftovers in the
fridge.
