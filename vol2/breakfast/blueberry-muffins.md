# Blueberry Muffins

## Ingredients


### Wet

- 2 ripe bananas, mashed
- 1 cup greek yogurt (nonfat or any type)
- 2 eggs
- 3 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract
- 2 cups blueberries (fresh or frozen)

### Dry

- 2 cups oat flour
- 2 scoops protein powder (≈1/2 cup)
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a large bowl, whisk together all dry ingredients
until evenly combined.
3. In a separate bowl, mash the bananas well.
4. Whisk in Greek yogurt, eggs, maple syrup, and
apple cider vinegar, vanilla until fully combined.
5. Mix the wet mixture into the dry ingredients just
until fully combined. Add 1 1/2 cups of blueberries
directly into the batter, saving the remaining 1/2 cup
for topping.
6. Line a muffin tin with liners and lightly spray with
avocado or olive oil spray. Optional, but it helps
keep them from sticking
7. Scoop batter evenly into 12 cups and top each
with a few of the remaining blueberries.
8. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until tops are golden
and a toothpick comes out clean.
9. Cool for 10 minutes before removing — this helps
them firm up and release from the muffin liners.
