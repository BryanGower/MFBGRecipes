# Low-Fat Granola

## Ingredients

- 2 bananas
- 3 cups of oats
- 3 scoops of vanilla protein powder (≈1
- cup)
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 2 tbsp of honey
- 2 tsp of cinnamon
- 1 tsp salt

### Low-Fat Granola


## Instructions

1. Preheat oven: Set oven to 350°F
(175°C) and line a baking sheet with
parchment paper.
2. Cook the bananas: Slice bananas
lengthwise. Heat 2 tbsp coconut oil in a
skillet over medium heat. Place banana
slices on the hot pan and cook a few
minutes per side until lightly golden and
softened. Mash into a smooth mixture.
3. Mix in dry: In a large bowl, combine
oats, cinnamon, salt, and protein powder.
4. Combine: Pour the warm mashed
banana mixture into the dry ingredients
and mix until evenly coated. Add honey
and stir until fully combined.
5. Bake: Spread the mixture on the
prepared baking sheet. Press it down
firmly to form clusters. Bake for 12–15
minutes, watching closely to make sure it
doesn’t burn.
6. Store: Let cool completely, then break
into pieces and store in a container.
