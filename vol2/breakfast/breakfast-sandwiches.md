# Breakfast Sandwiches

## Ingredients


### For The Biscuits


### Wet

- 5 eggs
- 1/4 cup parmesan, shreded(≈20g)
- 1/2 red onion, diced (≈1 cup)
- 1 red pepper, diced (≈1 cup)
- 1 jalapeño, diced (≈1/4 cup)
- 1/2 cup 2% cottage cheese

### Dry

- 2 cups oat flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- Seasonings: 1 tsp smoked paprika, 2 tsp
- garlic powder, 1/4 tsp cumin, 1/4 tsp
- oregano, 1/4 tsp thyme, 2 tsp salt, 1 tsp
- pepper

### Add-Ins For The Sandwich

- Breakfast sausage (turkey or any type)
- Optional:
- Avocado
- Spinach
- Cheese of choice
- Ketchup

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line a baking sheet with
parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together all wet ingredients until
fully combined.
3. In a separate bowl, whisk together all dry ingredients
until evenly mixed.
4. Combine the wet and dry mixtures and stir until just
combined.
5. Let the dough rest for 15 minutes to allow the oat flour
to absorb moisture.
6. Scoop the dough with an ice cream scoop into 7
evenly sized biscuits and place them on the lined baking
sheet.
7. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until tops are lightly golden and
firm to the touch.
8. Cook the breakfast sausage in a skillet over medium heat
until browned and fully cooked.
9. Slice biscuits in half, add the cooked sausage, and layer
with your choice of toppings — avocado, spinach, cheese, and
a drizzle of KA Ketchup.
NOTES:
Biscuits can be stored on the counter for 1–2 days, in the
fridge, or in the freezer. Toast them before serving for the
best texture and flavor.
