# Pumpkin Protein Bars

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups old-fashioned oats
- 4 scoops (≈+1 cup) vanilla protein powder
- 3/4 cup greek yogurt (nonfat or any type)
- 1/2 cup unsweetened pumpkin purée
- 1/4 cup peanut butter
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 2 tbsp melted coconut oil
- Optional: 1 tsp maple syrup

### Pumpkin Protein Bars

- 1 3 g 7 g 1 0 g 1 5 9

## Instructions

1. Mix the Base:
Combine all base ingredients in a bowl
until smooth and fully mixed.
2. Press into Pan:
Line a baking dish with parchment paper
and press the mixture evenly into it. Use
a 9×9 pan for thicker bars or a 9×13 pan
for thinner bars.
3. Make the Chocolate Sauce:
In a small bowl, whisk cocoa powder,
coconut oil, and maple syrup (if using).
4. Add the Topping:
Spread the chocolate sauce over the base,
then freeze for 30 minutes to let them
harden. Cut into bars, eat, and store the rest
in the freezer for best texture.
