# Strawberry Bowl

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup strawberries (optional if
- using strawberry protein powder)
- 1 banana
- 1 cup greek yogurt (nonfat or any
- type)
- 1 scoop strawberry or vanilla
- protein powder
- 2 tbsp chia seeds
- Pinch of salt

### Strawberry Pudding

- 2 8 g 5 g 2 3 g 2 5 5 P O S T -  5 0 M I N S 2

## Instructions

1. Blend the banana, Greek yogurt,
chia seeds, protein powder, and pinch
of salt until smooth. If using vanilla
protein powder, add the strawberries
before blending. If using strawberry
protein powder, you can skip the fresh
strawberries.
2. Pour into a bowl and chill in the
fridge for 45 minutes or overnight
to set.
3. Top with fresh berries or any
toppings of your choice.
