# Pumpkin Pudding

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup of unsweetened pumpkin puree
- 1 banana
- 1 cup of greek yogurt (nonfat or any
- type)
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 2 tbsp chia seeds
- Pinch of salt

### Pumpkin Pudding

## Instructions

1. Blend the pumpkin purée, banana, greek
yogurt, vanilla protein powder, chia seeds, and
a pinch of salt in a blender until smooth, thick,
and fully combined.
2. Pour the mixture into a bowl or jar and
place it in the fridge for at least 45 minutes, or
overnight, to let it chill and thicken into a
pudding consistency.
3. Top with cinnamon, fruit, or any
toppings you like right before serving
for extra flavor and texture.
