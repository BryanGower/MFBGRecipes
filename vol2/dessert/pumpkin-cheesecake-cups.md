# Pumpkin Cheesecake Cups

## Ingredients


### Base Layer

- 1 cup oat flour
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (nonfat)
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (≈1/3
- cup)
- Pinch of salt
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon

### Cheesecake Filling

- 1/2 cup cottage cheese (2%)
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (nonfat)
- 3/4 cup unsweetened pumpkin purée
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon

### Chocolate Sauce

- 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 tbsp melted coconut oil
- 1 tsp maple syrup

## Instructions

1. Make the Base:
Mix all base layer ingredients in a bowl. Shape into
small cups on a parchment-lined tray. Stick them
in the freezer to harden while making the filling.
2. Make the Filling:
Blend cottage cheese, greek yogurt, pumpkin
purée, vanilla, maple syrup, and cinnamon until
smooth. Pour into the cups. Freeze until firm
(about 20 minutes).
3. Make the Chocolate Sauce:
Mix cocoa powder, melted coconut oil, and
maple syrup until smooth.
4. Assemble:
Once the cheesecake cups are firm, drizzle with
chocolate sauce. Return to the freezer to set
(about 20 minutes).
5. Eat & Store:
Enjoy immediately and store the rest in the
fridge.
