# Banana Bread

## Ingredients


### Dry

- 3/4 cup almond flour
- 1 cup vanilla protein powder (≈3 scoops)
- 1/2 cup walnuts (≈50g)
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp salt

### Wet

- 3 ripe bananas (≈300 g)
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup greek yogurt (nonfat or any type)
- 2 tbsp maple syrup

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 300°F (175°C).
2. In a bowl, mix all dry ingredients.
3. In a separate bowl, mash the
bananas until smooth and mix in all the
other wet ingredients.
4. Combine wet and dry ingredients -
mix thoroughly.
5. Pour the batter into a greased or
lined loaf pan
6. Bake for 1 hour at 300°F
7. Remove from the oven, let cool,
slice and enjoy.
