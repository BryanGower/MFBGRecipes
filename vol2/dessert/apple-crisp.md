# Apple Crisp

## Ingredients


### Instructions


### Crisp Mixture

- 1 3/4 cups old-fashioned oats
- 2 tbsp honey
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- 1/2 cup walnuts (≈45g)
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp salt

### Apple Mixture

- 3 large apples (Honeycrisp)
- 2 tsp honey
- 2 tsp maple syrup
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp vanilla
- Optional: 1 tsp lemon zest
