# Berry Crisp

## Ingredients

- 4 g

### Crisp Mixture

- 1 3/4 cups old-fashioned oats
- 2 tbsp honey
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- 1/2 cup walnuts (≈45g)
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp salt

### Berry Mixture

- 4 cups blackberries (or berries of
- choice)
- 2 tsp maple syrup
- 2 tsp honey
- Optional: 1 tbsp juice + 1 tsp lemon
- zest

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. In a food processor, add all the base
ingredients and process until combined.
3. Press half of the mixture firmly into the
bottom of a 9×9 baking dish.
4. In a separate bowl, mix together all the
berry mixture ingredients. Spread evenly
over the base.
5. Bake 20–25 minutes, until golden.
6. Remove from oven, let cool completely, slice,
and enjoy.
NOTES:
If you want a crispier top, divide the crisp
mixture in half and mix 1 teaspoon of melted
coconut oil into the top layer.
For extra protein, top it with Greek yogurt.
