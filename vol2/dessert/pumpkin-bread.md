# Pumpkin Bread

## Ingredients


### Dry

- 3/4 cup almond flour
- 1 cup vanilla protein powder (≈3
- scoops)
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp of salt

### Wet

- 1 1/2 cups pumpkin puree (1 can)
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup geek yogurt (5% or any type)
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp of pure vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 300°F (175°C).
2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together all
the dry ingredients.
3. In a separate bowl add and mix the wet
ingredients.
4. Pour the wet mixture into the dry
ingredients. Mix until fully combined.
5. Pour the batter into a greased or lined loaf
pan.
6. Bake for 1 hour
7. Remove from the oven, let cool completely,
slice and enjoy.
