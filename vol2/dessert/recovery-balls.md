# Recovery Balls

## Ingredients

- 2 cups old-fashioned oats
- 2 scoops vanilla protein
- powder
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (2% or
- any type)
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 3 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 1/2 tbsp honey
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa
- powder (for rolling)

## Instructions

1. Add oats, protein powder, greek yogurt,
almond butter, chia seeds, honey, and salt
to a food processor.
2. Blend until fully combined into a thick,
sticky dough.
3. Roll mixture into 8 balls.
4. Place cocoa powder in a small bowl
and roll each ball to coat.
5. Transfer to a parchment-lined
container and store in the fridge.
