# Banana Pudding

## Ingredients

- 1 banana
- 1 cup of greek yogurt (nonfat
- or any type)
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 2 tbsp chia seeds
- Pinch of salt
- 2 7 g 5 g 2 0 g 2 4 3

## Instructions

1. Blend the banana, Greek yogurt,
vanilla protein powder, chia seeds,
and a pinch of salt together in a
blender until smooth and creamy.
2. Pour the mixture into a bowl or jar
and place in the fridge to chill for at
least 45 minutes, or overnight, so it
thickens into a pudding consistency.
3. Top with sliced banana, oats, or any
fruit or toppings you like right before
serving.
