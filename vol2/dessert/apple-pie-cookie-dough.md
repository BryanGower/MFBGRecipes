# Apple Pie Cookie Dough

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
- 1/2 cup oat flour
- 2 scoops vanilla protein powder
- (≈3/4 cup)
- 1 large Honeycrisp apple, diced
- very small (≈2 cups)
- 1/4 cup greek yogurt (nonfat or
- any type)
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp lemon zest (optional)
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tsp honey
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. Heat a pan over medium-high.
Add apples, maple syrup, cinnamon,
salt and optional lemon zest. Cook
5–7 min until softened. Cool slightly.
2. In a bowl, mix oats, oat flour,
protein powder, honey, yogurt, and
lemon juice until thick + sticky.
3. Mix in cooked apples.
4. Transfer to a container, cover, and
store in the fridge.
5. Keep refrigerated and enjoy within
5 days.
