# Chocolate Chips

## Ingredients

- 1 cup coconut oil
- 2 cups unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. Mix all the ingredients together.
It should be a thick texture, if it isn’t,
add more cocoa powder and adjust its
sweetness with the maple syrup.
2. Freeze for a few hours until solid.
3. Cut the block of chocolate into
pieces with a sharp knife.
4. Store in the fridge or freezer
after using.
