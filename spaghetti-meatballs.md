# Harvest Soup

## Ingredients

- 1 cup black rice
- 1 lb chicken breast (≈3 cups, shredded)
- 2 cups carrots, peeled and sliced into rounds
- 2 cups butternut squash, cubed
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 cup celery (about 2 stalks), chopped
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 cups mushrooms, chopped
- 5 cups broth (chicken or vegetable)
- 3 cups coconut milk (from the refrigerated
- section)
- 1 tbsp olive oil (for sautéing)

### Seasonings

- 1 tbsp fresh thyme (2–3 sprigs)
- 1 tbsp fresh rosemary
- 1 1/2 tsp dried sage
- 2 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp salt (adjust as you go)
- 1/2 tsp black pepper

## Instructions

1. Prepare your shredded chicken to your liking
and set aside.
2. Cook the rice: Add black rice and salted water
to a pot. Bring to a boil, cover, reduce to a simmer,
and cook for about 40 minutes until tender.
3. Sauté the vegetables: While the rice cooks, heat
olive oil in a large soup pot over medium-high heat.
Add carrots, squash, onion, and celery. Sauté for
about 10 minutes, stirring often, until softened.
4. Add seasonings: Stir in garlic, thyme, rosemary,
sage, paprika, cayenne, salt, and pepper. Cook for
2–3 minutes until fragrant.
5. Cook mushrooms: Add mushrooms and sauté
another 5 minutes until they release liquid and soften.
6. Simmer the soup: Pour in broth, then add the
cooked rice and shredded chicken. Bring to a simmer,
cover, and cook for 30 minutes to let flavors combine.
7. Finish with coconut milk: Stir in coconut milk,
simmer another 5 minutes, then taste and adjust
seasonings.
8. Serve: Ladle hot soup into bowls and enjoy
immediately.
