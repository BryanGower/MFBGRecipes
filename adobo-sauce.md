# Mashed Sweet Potatoes

## Ingredients

- 2 large sweet potatoes (or any type)
- 1/2 cups parmesan cheese, shredded
- 1 cup cottage cheese (2% or any type)
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper

## Instructions

1. Prep the sweet potatoes:
Peel the potatoes and cut into chunks.
2. Boil:
Place potatoes into a large pot of boiling
water. Cook for 15-20 minutes, or until
potatoes are completely tender (test with
a fork).
3. Drain:
Remove potatoes from boiling water and
drain well.
4. Blend:
Add the potatoes, parmesan, garlic, salt,
and pepper into a blender or food
processor. Blend until completely smooth
and creamy.
5. Serve:
Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
