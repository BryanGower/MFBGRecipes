# Egg Hashbrown Skillet

## Ingredients

- 2 large potatoes (about 2 1/2 cups
- shredded)
- 6 eggs
- 1 yellow onion
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F. Place the oil in
a cast iron skillet and heat it in the oven until
hot.
2. Peel and shred the potatoes, then squeeze
out excess moisture using a cheesecloth or
towel.
3. Mix the shredded potatoes with paprika,
garlic powder, black pepper, and salt.
4. Carefully remove the skillet from the oven.
Spread the potatoes evenly in the skillet,
pressing them down slightly. Top with sliced
onions.
5. Bake for 25 minutes until golden and crispy.
6. Crack the eggs on top of the hashbrowns
and sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
7. Bake for another 7–10 minutes until the
eggs are cooked to your liking.
8. Sprinkle with extra salt if needed.
