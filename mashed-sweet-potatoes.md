# Sweet Potato Quiche

## Ingredients


### The Crust

- 1 medium sweet potato (thinly sliced rounds)

### The Vegetable Filling

- 1 cup onion, diced
- 1 cup mushrooms, diced
- 1 handful spinach
- 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 tsp paprika
- Pinch of salt and pepper

### The Egg Mixture

- 5 eggs
- 3/4 cup cottage cheese (2%)
- 1/2 cup parmesan, shredded
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Chop & Layer:
Add a little olive oil to a pie pan. Slice the
sweet potato into very thin rounds (you may
not use the whole potato). Overlap and stack
slices to fully cover the pie pan, pressing
them up the sides. It will look thick, but
they’ll thin out once baked.
3. Bake the Crust:
Bake 10–15 minutes until partially cooked and
flexible. Readjust slices up the sides if
needed. Optional: place another pie pan on
top while baking to keep slices in place.
4. Sauté onions, garlic, salt, pepper, and paprika in
olive oil until softened. Add mushrooms and cherry
tomatoes, cook a few minutes, then stir in spinach
until wilted.
5. Combine eggs, cottage cheese, parmesan, salt,
and pepper — whisk or blend until smooth.
6. Mix the sautéed vegetables into the egg mixture,
ensuring they're evenly combined. Pour the mixture
over the pre-baked sweet potato crust and gently
spread so the vegetables are distributed throughout.
7. Bake for 30 minutes, or until the center is set.
8. Cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
