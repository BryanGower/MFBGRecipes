# Tomato Soup

## Ingredients

- 12 roma tomatoes (≈8 cups), diced
- 1 yellow onion, diced
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can white beans (Cannellini), drained and rinsed
- 1 1/2 cups cottage cheese (2%)
- 3 cups broth (chicken or vegetable)
- 1 tbsp olive oil

### Seasonings

- 2 tsp oregano
- 2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- 2 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional for spice)

## Instructions

1. Prep: Dice the onion and tomatoes. Mince the
garlic.
2. Sauté: Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium
heat. Add onion and garlic, and cook until translucent
and fragrant, about 3–4 minutes.
3. Add tomatoes + spices: Add diced tomatoes,
oregano, basil, salt, pepper, paprika, and cayenne. Stir
and cook until the tomatoes soften, about 5–7
minutes.
4. Simmer: Add the beans and broth, stir well, and let
it simmer for about 20 minutes so the flavors
combine.
5. Blend until smooth: Turn off the heat. Transfer
the soup to a blender (or use an immersion
blender) and blend until smooth.
6. Add cottage cheese (without curdling):
In a blender, combine 1 cup of hot soup with all of
the cottage cheese and blend until completely
smooth. Let the main soup cool for about 5 minutes
off heat, then slowly stir in the blended cottage
cheese mixture. Do not boil again; if needed, warm
gently over low heat.
If it separates slightly, re-blend gently — it’ll return
to a smooth texture.
7. Serve: Ladle into bowls and top with fresh basil
and cracked pepper.
