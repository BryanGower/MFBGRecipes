# Ranch

## Ingredients


### Instructions

- 1/2 cup cottage cheese (4%
- or any type)
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt (5% or
- any type)
- 1 garlic clove
- 2 tbsp fresh dill
- 1 tbsp green onion
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/4 salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- Water (if needed for texture)

## Instructions

1. Add everything into a blender,
making sure the yogurt and cottage
cheese go in first so it blends
easier.
2. Blend until completely smooth.
3. Taste and adjust. If the sauce
is too thick, add a small splash of
water or more lemon juice juice
and blend again until it reaches
your preferred consistency.
