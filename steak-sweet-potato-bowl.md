# Tacos

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cottage cheese (4% fat)
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- Pinch of salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (177°C).
2. Blend cottage cheese, eggs, paprika,
garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Pour mixture into 5-6 small tacoshaped circles on a parchment-lined
baking sheet.
4. Bake for 15–20 minutes. Watch so
they don’t burn.
5. Cool for a few minutes before adding
toppings.
