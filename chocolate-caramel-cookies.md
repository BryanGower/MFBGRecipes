# Blackberry Apple Pie Cookies

## Ingredients

- 2 large Honeycrisp apples (about 4 cups,
- diced)
- 3 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp Cinnamon
- 3 scoops vanilla protein powder (1 cup)
- 1 cup old-fashioned oats
- 1 cup oat flour
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp honey
- 2 cups blackberries (fresh or frozen)
- Pinch of salt
- Optional (for texture):
- 1/4 cup milk (any kind — add only if the
- mixture is too dry)

## Instructions

BLACKBERRY APPLE PIE COOKIES INSTRUCTIONS 1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line a
baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Cook apples:
Heat and mix diced apples with maple
syrup, cinnamon, and salt for 6–8 min
until soft.
3. Make dough:
Mix protein powder, oat flour, oats,
greek yogurt, honey, and blackberries
in a bowl.
4. Combine:
Fold in the apples. Scoop into 10 balls and
place on the baking sheet.
5. Bake for 12 minutes.

## Notes

Don’t over-bake.
Pull them out while still soft-ish! Store in a
container on the counter (they’ll get moist
after a day) or freeze and microwave for 10–
15 seconds.
If overly dry — add a splash of milk.
If overly wet — add a bit more oat flour.
