# Chicken Patties

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground chicken
- 1/2 red onion
- 1/2 red bell pepper
- 1/2 cup corn
- 1/4 cup cilantro
- 2 minced garlic cloves
- 2 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 tsp ceyenne pepper
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper

### Chicken Pattys


## Instructions

1. Chop onion, and bell peppers. Dice cilantro
and garlic.
2. Combine ground chicken, onion, bell pepper,
corn, cilantro garlic and all spices in large bowl.
Mix ingredients until evenly combined.
3. Form patties into 5 equal sized balls.
Flatten slightly for even cooking.
4. On stovetop place a little olive oil in a
skillet over medium heat. Cook 5–6
minutes per side until golden and cooked
through.
