--- name: "Tahini Sauce" category: "Sauces" volume: 2 time: "15 min" servings: "6" --- # Tahini Sauce ## Ingredients - 1/4 cup tahini - 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar - 1 1/2 tbsp honey - 1/4 cup greek yogurt (nonfat) - 2 garlic cloves, minced - 1/4 tsp salt - A few cracks of black pepper - Water (if needed for texture) ## Instructions 1. Mince the garlic and add all ingredients to a bowl. 2. Mix until smooth and creamy. 3. Thin with water, a little at a time, until you reach your desired consistency.
