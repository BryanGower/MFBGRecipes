# Cottage Cheese Chips

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cottage cheese 2% (Good
- Culture works well)
- Seasonings:
- (paprika, garlic powder, onion
- powder, Everything But The
- Bagel, etc.)

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F
(175°C).
2. Line a baking sheet with
parchment paper.
3. Spoon the cottage cheese into
individual portions (about 15
spoonfuls/balls) and place them on
the parchment-lined baking sheet.
4. Add your desired seasonings on
top of each portion of cottage
cheese.
5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, keeping
an eye on them to avoid burning.
6. Let them cool slightly before
peeling them off of the parchment
paper.
